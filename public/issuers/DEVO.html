Overview

Devo is a cybersecurity unicorn providing a cloud-native security data platform that enables enterprises to perform real-time threat detection, investigation, and response at massive scale. The company's logging and security analytics solution ingests and analyzes petabytes of security data in real-time, helping security operations centers (SOCs) detect threats faster than traditional SIEM solutions. Devo serves Fortune 500 enterprises and government agencies with its elastic, scalable architecture that eliminates data silos and provides sub-second query performance across years of historical data. Named a Leader in the 2024 IDC MarketScape Worldwide SIEM assessment.
