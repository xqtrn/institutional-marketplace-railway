Overview

Alto Pharmacy is a full-service digital pharmacy founded in 2015 by Jamie Karraker and Matt Gamache-Asselin, former Facebook employees. The company combines AI-powered technology with same-day courier delivery to improve medication access and adherence. Alto started by acquiring a small pharmacy in San Francisco's Mission District and has since expanded to 13 markets with over 500,000 patients. The company crossed $1 billion in revenue in 2022 and raised $120M in September 2024 at $800M valuation. CEO Alicia Boler Davis (former Amazon SVP of Global Customer Fulfillment) joined in 2022. In May 2025, Alto was acquired by Fuze Health for $1.25B. The platform integrates with physicians, insurers, and pharmaceutical manufacturers to provide transparent pricing and medication management.
