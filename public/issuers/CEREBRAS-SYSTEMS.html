Overview

Cerebras Systems is pioneering the future of AI computing with the world's largest and fastest AI chips. The company's Wafer-Scale Engine (WSE) technology uses an entire silicon wafer to create a single massive chip with 900,000 AI-optimized cores and 4 trillion transistors, making it 56 times larger than the biggest GPU. Cerebras serves leading research institutions, government agencies, and enterprises including AWS, Meta, and IBM, enabling breakthrough AI research and production deployments. The WSE-3 was named TIME's Best Invention of 2024 in the AI category.
