Overview

Insitro is an AI-first drug discovery company founded in 2018 by Daphne Koller, Stanford professor and Coursera co-founder. The company uses machine learning to redefine disease biology and accelerate drug development. Insitro raised $643M+ at $2.5B valuation from investors including T. Rowe Price, ARCH Ventures, Andreessen Horowitz, and Jeff Bezos. Major pharma partnerships include Gilead (metabolic diseases) and Bristol Myers Squibb (NASH, ALS). CEO Koller was a 2004 MacArthur 'genius' grant recipient.
