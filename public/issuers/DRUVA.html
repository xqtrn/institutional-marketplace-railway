Overview

Druva is the leading SaaS platform for cloud data protection, enabling organizations to backup, recover, and govern their data across endpoints, cloud applications, and data center workloads. Founded in 2008 by Jaspreet Singh, Milind Borate, and Ramani Kothandaraman, Druva has grown to serve over 6,000 customers worldwide with $304M in annual revenue. The platform leverages AI and machine learning to provide real-time insights, security threat detection, and compliance monitoring. As a 100% SaaS solution, Druva eliminates the need for hardware infrastructure while delivering industry-leading RPO and RTO guarantees. The company achieved unicorn status in 2019 and continues to lead the cloud data protection market.
