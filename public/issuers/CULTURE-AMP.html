Overview

Culture Amp is the world's leading employee experience platform, founded in 2009 in Melbourne by Didier Elzinga, Jon Williams, Douglas English, and Rod Hamilton. The platform helps organizations understand and improve employee engagement, performance, and retention through AI-powered surveys, analytics, and action tools. Culture Amp serves over 6,500 companies globally including Salesforce, Slack, Lyft, and Airbnb. The company achieved unicorn status in 2021 with a $1.5B valuation and continues to expand through strategic acquisitions including Orgnostic in 2024. A Forrester Total Economic Impact study found enterprise customers achieved 311% ROI with the platform.
