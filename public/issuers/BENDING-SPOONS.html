Overview

Bending Spoons is an Italian technology conglomerate founded in 2013 and headquartered in Milan. The company develops and acquires consumer-focused digital products including Evernote, Remini, Splice, Meetup, WeTransfer, and Issuu. Known for its acquisition strategy, Bending Spoons optimizes acquired products for profitability, reaching over 500 million downloads. The company raised $155M in February 2024 at a $2.55B valuation and completed 6 major acquisitions in 2024 alone, including WeTransfer, Issuu, and Hopin/StreamYard.
