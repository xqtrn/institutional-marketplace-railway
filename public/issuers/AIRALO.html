Overview

Airalo is the world's first eSIM marketplace and the first unicorn in the eSIM industry. Founded in 2019 by Ahmet Bahadir Ozdemir (CEO) and Abraham Burak (COO) during Antler's Southeast Asia residency, the company enables travelers to instantly activate phone eSIMs with talk, text, and data plans from carriers worldwide at lower rates than traditional roaming. With operational bases in Singapore and Toronto and headquarters in Delaware, Airalo serves over 20 million travelers across 200+ destinations. In July 2025, the company achieved unicorn status with a $220M Series C led by CVC (CVC Asia Fund VI), with participation from telecom strategic investors including Peak XV (formerly Sequoia India), Bell Ventures, Orange Ventures, TelefÃ³nica, Singtel Innov8, and Rakuten Capital. Airalo operates with a fully remote team across 55 countries, exemplifying the new generation of globally distributed tech startups.
