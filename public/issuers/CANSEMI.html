Overview

CanSemi Technology (Guangzhou CanSemi) operates Guangdong Province's first 12-inch wafer fabrication facility, a strategic asset in China's push for semiconductor self-sufficiency. Founded in 2017 and backed by major automotive OEMs including GAC, SAIC, and BAIC, the company specializes in analog ICs for automotive, industrial, and 5G applications. In December 2024, CanSemi launched production at its CNY 16.25 billion Phase III facility, adding 40,000 wafers per month and bringing total capacity to 80,000 12-inch wafers monthly. The fab utilizes 180-90nm technology with six ASML lithography machines, producing power devices, PMICs, MCUs, and image sensors. With over CNY 37 billion invested across three phases and plans for a fourth phase, CanSemi is establishing Guangzhou as a major semiconductor manufacturing hub.
