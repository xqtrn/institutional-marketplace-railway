Overview

Beamery is the leading AI-powered talent lifecycle management platform used by enterprise organizations including General Motors, VMware, Johnson & Johnson, Salesforce, and Amazon. Founded in 2013 by Abakar Saidov, Sultan Saidov, and Mike Paterson in London, Beamery achieved unicorn status in 2022. The platform provides intelligence for workforce planning, recruiting, talent mobility, and skills-based hiring. In 2024, Beamery's revenue reached $112.8M with customers seeing 40% reduction in time-to-hire and 4x increase in human productivity. The company is a founding signatory of the European Commission's AI Pact for ethical AI.
