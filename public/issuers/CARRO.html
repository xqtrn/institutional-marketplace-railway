Overview

Carro is Southeast Asia's largest automotive marketplace and Singapore's leading unicorn in the used car space. Founded in 2015 by CMU alumni Aaron Tan, Aditya Lesmana, and Kelvin Chng, the platform offers end-to-end car buying and selling with integrated financing, insurance, and after-sales services. Carro operates in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Taiwan, Indonesia, Japan, and Hong Kong. The company achieved first operating profit in 2024 with 11x YoY EBITDA growth. Backed by SoftBank Vision Fund, Temasek, and EDBI, Carro is preparing for a US IPO targeting $3B+ valuation in 2026.
