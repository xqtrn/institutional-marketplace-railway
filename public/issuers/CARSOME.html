Overview

Carsome is Malaysia's first tech unicorn and Southeast Asia's largest integrated used car platform by revenue and transaction volume. Founded in 2015 by Eric Cheng and Jiun Ee Teoh, the platform enables consumers to buy and sell used cars across Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Singapore, and the Philippines. Carsome reached unicorn status in 2021 and raised $290M Series E in 2022 at $1.7B valuation. The company achieved its first quarterly profit in Q2 2024 with over $310M revenue and is preparing for a dual listing on Singapore and US exchanges.
