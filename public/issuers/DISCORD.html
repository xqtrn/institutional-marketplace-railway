Overview

Discord is a communication platform founded in 2015 by Jason Citron and Stanislav Vishnevsky. Originally designed for gamers, Discord has expanded to serve communities across education, crypto, music, and more. The platform reaches over 200 million monthly users across 60,000+ game titles (1.5B hours of gameplay monthly). Discord raised $600M at $15B valuation in 2021 and generated $879M revenue in 2024. Microsoft attempted to acquire Discord for $12B in 2021 but the deal fell through. The company began testing ads in 2024 as it expands beyond gaming.
