Overview

BigPanda is the pioneering leader in AI-powered IT operations (AIOps), helping enterprises transform reactive and manual incident management into intelligent, automated workflows. Founded in 2012 by former Sequoia investor Assaf Resnick, BigPanda's platform uses machine learning to correlate alerts from monitoring tools, identify root causes, and orchestrate rapid incident resolution. The platform integrates with 100+ tools including Datadog, ServiceNow, PagerDuty, and Slack. BigPanda serves hundreds of Fortune 500 enterprises, helping reduce alert noise by up to 99% and cut MTTR by 50%. The company launched Agentic IT Operations in 2025 to bring autonomous AI to the $200 billion ITOps market.
