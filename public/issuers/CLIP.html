Overview

Clip is Mexico's largest digital payments and commerce enablement platform, serving over one million small and mid-sized businesses since its founding in 2012. The company provides a complete portfolio of purpose-built payments, financial services, and software solutions enabling SMBs to accept card payments, access working capital, and manage their business operations. Clip became Mexico's first payments unicorn in 2021 with a $2 billion valuation and maintains profitability in its core payment services, which represent over 85% of its business. The company continues to expand its market presence while driving financial inclusion across Mexico.
