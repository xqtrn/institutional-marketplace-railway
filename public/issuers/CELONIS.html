Overview

Celonis is a process mining and execution management company founded in 2011 by Bastian Nominacher, Alexander Rinke, and Martin Klenk in Munich, Germany. The company's Process Intelligence platform analyzes business processes from enterprise systems to identify inefficiencies and drive automation. Celonis raised $400M at $13B valuation in August 2022 led by Qatar Investment Authority. ARR grew to $771M in 2023 (up 39% from $400M in 2021). Celonis ranks #12 on Forbes Cloud 100 2025 and is named a Leader in Gartner Magic Quadrant for Process Mining.
