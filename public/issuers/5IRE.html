Overview

5ire is a fifth-generation blockchain ecosystem built around the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals. Founded by Indian entrepreneurs Pratik Gauri and Prateek Dwivedi in 2021, the company became India's fastest-growing blockchain unicorn with $100M Series A at $1.5B valuation from SRAM & MRAM. Their unique Proof-of-Benefit consensus mechanism rewards validators based on sustainability practices and ESG scores. Launched mainnet in May 2024 with $10M grant program.
