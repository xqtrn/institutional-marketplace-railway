Overview

Animoca Brands is the global leader in blockchain gaming and Web3 investments, founded in 2014 and headquartered in Hong Kong. A Deloitte Tech Fast winner and Fortune Crypto 40 company, Animoca Brands drives digital property rights through the open metaverse. In 2024, the company reported $314M in bookings with $4.3B in total assets. In November 2025, Animoca announced plans for a Nasdaq listing via reverse merger with Currenc Group at a $2.4B valuation. Key subsidiaries include The Sandbox, Moca Network, and nWay. The company holds 540+ portfolio investments across AI, Web3, DeFi, and gaming.
