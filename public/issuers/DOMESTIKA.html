Overview

Domestika is the world's largest creative community and online learning platform for creative professionals, valued at $1.3 billion. Originally founded in Madrid in 2002 and now headquartered in Berkeley, California, Domestika offers professionally-produced courses taught by top creative experts in fields including illustration, design, photography, crafts, and more. The platform distinguishes itself through high production values, affordable pricing, and a strong community of creators. Domestika achieved unicorn status in January 2022 after raising $110 million in Series D funding led by Zeev Ventures. The platform serves millions of students globally with courses available in multiple languages. The company expanded through the acquisition of CGMA, an art and design education platform, in 2022.
