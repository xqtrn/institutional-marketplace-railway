Overview

Bilt Rewards is a fintech company that created the first loyalty program allowing renters to earn points on rent payments. Founded in 2021 by Ankur Jain through his startup studio Kairos, Bilt has transformed how Americans think about their largest monthly expense. Members can earn points on rent and mortgage payments through ACH, debit, or credit card methods, then transfer them 1:1 to 20+ airline and hotel partners including American Airlines, United, Hyatt, and IHG. Bilt reached a $10.8 billion valuation in July 2025 after raising $250 million, becoming one of the fastest-growing fintech companies in history. Revenue grew from $113M in 2023 to $400M+ in Q1 2025, with the company targeting $1 billion by Q1 2026. Ken Chenault, former AmEx CEO, joined as Chairman.
