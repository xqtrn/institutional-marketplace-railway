Overview

Chehaoduo Group is China's largest online car trading platform, operating Guazi.com for used vehicles and Maodou for new cars. Founded in 2014 by Mark Haoyong Yang, who previously founded classified site Ganji, the company pioneered the consumer-to-consumer (C2C) model for used car transactions in China, eliminating middlemen and streamlining the buying process. Backed by SoftBank Vision Fund with nearly $4 billion in total funding, Chehaoduo has grown to become a comprehensive automotive services platform offering vehicle inspections, financing, insurance, and extended warranties. The company was considering a US IPO in 2024 at a $10.5 billion valuation.
