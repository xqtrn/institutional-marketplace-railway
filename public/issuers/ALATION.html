Overview

Alation is the leading data intelligence company enabling enterprise organizations to realize value from their data. Founded in 2012 by Satyen Sangani, Aaron Kalb, Feng Nui, and Venky Ganti, Alation pioneered the data catalog market. The platform combines machine learning with human insights for data discovery, governance, and analytics. Raised $123M Series E in November 2022 at $1.7B valuation led by Thoma Bravo. ARR reached $109M in 2024 with 727 employees and offices in Redwood City, London, and Chennai serving Fortune 500 customers globally.
