Overview

At-Bay is a cyber insurance company that uses technology to transform how businesses protect themselves against cyber risk. Founded in 2016, the company combines insurance with proactive security services to help businesses prevent cyberattacks before they happen. At-Bay's approach integrates security monitoring, risk assessment, and insurance coverage into a unified platform, providing policyholders with continuous visibility into their cyber risk posture while delivering competitive insurance pricing based on actual security performance.
