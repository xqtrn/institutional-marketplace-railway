Overview

Du Xiaoman Financial (formerly Baidu Financial Services Group) is one of China's largest independent fintech platforms, spun off from Baidu in 2018 in a $1.9 billion transaction. The company leverages Baidu's advanced AI and big data capabilities to provide comprehensive financial services including consumer credit, wealth management, and digital banking solutions. With over 330 million credited users and CNY 500 billion in cumulative loans facilitated, Du Xiaoman has become a cornerstone of China's digital finance ecosystem. The platform partners with over 100 financial institutions and continues to innovate in AI-driven risk assessment and personalized financial services.
