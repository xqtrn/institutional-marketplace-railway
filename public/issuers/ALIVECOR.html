Overview

AliveCor is a digital health company pioneering AI-powered personal electrocardiogram (ECG) technology. Founded in 2011 by Dr. David Albert, a physician and serial inventor, AliveCor developed KardiaMobile, the first FDA-cleared personal ECG device. The company has built the world's largest AI-driven consumer subscription service for cardiovascular care. In June 2024, AliveCor received dual FDA clearance for KAI 12L AI technology detecting 35 cardiac conditions including heart attacks, and the Kardia 12L ECG Systemâ€”the world's first AI-powered handheld 12-lead ECG. AliveCor's technology has been trained on over 1.75 million ECGs. The company won ITC exclusion order against Apple and Inc.'s 2024 Best in Business Award for innovation.
