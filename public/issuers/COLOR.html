Overview

Color Health is a healthcare technology company transforming cancer care through population-scale genetic testing, virtual cancer clinics, and AI-powered care navigation. Founded in 2013, the company pivoted from genetic testing during COVID-19 to become a leader in COVID testing and vaccine coordination, then evolved into a comprehensive cancer screening and early detection platform. Color's virtual clinic assesses cancer risk, increases screening accessibility, and accelerates diagnosis for employers and health plans. The company reached a $4.6B valuation in 2021 and has since partnered with OpenAI (for cancer copilot AI) and Google Cloud (for breast cancer screening expansion). Color serves large employers and government programs with end-to-end cancer care solutions.
