Overview

ConsenSys is the leading Web3 software company building foundational infrastructure for the decentralized internet, including MetaMask (the world's most popular self-custodial crypto wallet with 30M+ monthly active users), Infura (API platform powering thousands of dApps), and Linea (zkEVM Layer 2 rollup). Founded by Joseph Lubin, Ethereum co-founder, ConsenSys has raised $715M from top investors including Microsoft, Temasek, SoftBank, and ParaFi Capital. The company is reportedly preparing for an IPO with JPMorgan and Goldman Sachs as advisors and continues to expand MetaMask with perpetual trading, rewards programs, and the upcoming MASK token.
