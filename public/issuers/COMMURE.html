Overview

Commure is a healthcare operating system company that merged with Athelas in October 2023 to create a $6B healthcare infrastructure platform serving over 130 health systems. The company's CommureOS platform unifies clinical AI, workflow automation, revenue cycle management, and patient engagement tools. Commure has made strategic acquisitions including Augmedix ($139M, 2024) for AI medical documentation and Memora Health for digital care navigation. Backed by General Catalyst, Sequoia, and leading healthcare investors, Commure is building the definitive operating layer for healthcare organizations.
