Overview

CATL Intelligent Mining is the smart mining solutions division of Contemporary Amperex Technology Co. Limited (CATL), the world's largest EV battery manufacturer. Through strategic partnerships including EACON Mining Technology, CATL provides battery systems optimized for extreme mining conditions, enabling electric autonomous haulage trucks to operate in high altitudes, freezing temperatures, and rugged terrains. As of September 2025, over 2,000 electric autonomous mining trucks powered by CATL technology have covered 65 million kilometers across coal, copper, limestone, and gold mining operations. CATL has also partnered with BHP to explore electrification solutions for global mining operations.
