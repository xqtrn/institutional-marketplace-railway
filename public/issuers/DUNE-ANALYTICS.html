Overview

Dune Analytics is the leading community-powered blockchain data analytics platform, enabling users to query, visualize, and share insights from on-chain data across Ethereum, Bitcoin, Solana, and 20+ other blockchains. Founded in 2018 in Oslo, Norway by Fredrik Haga and Mats Olsen, Dune became a unicorn in 2022 after raising $69.2 million at a $1 billion valuation. The platform hosts over 700,000 community-created dashboards tracking DeFi protocols, NFT markets, and blockchain activity. In 2024, Dune expanded through a Snowflake partnership to distribute blockchain data to enterprise customers, and acquired smlXL to enhance its data infrastructure capabilities.
