Overview

Checkout.com is a global payments infrastructure company founded in 2012 by Guillaume Pousaz. The company provides payment gateway, acquiring, and processing services for enterprise clients. In September 2025, Checkout.com announced a $12B internal valuation (up 30% from $9.35B in 2023 but down from $40B peak in 2022). The company achieved profitability in 2024 with 45% net revenue growth and processes over $1B daily. Major clients include Netflix, Shein, Grab, Alibaba, Sony, and eBay.
