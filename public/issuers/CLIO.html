Overview

Clio is the world's leading cloud-based legal practice management platform, used by over 150,000 legal professionals across 130+ countries. Founded in 2008, Clio simplifies law firm operations by centralizing client intake, case management, document management, billing, and payments in one platform. The company has raised record-setting funding for legal tech, including a $900M Series F at a $3B valuation in 2024, followed by $500M that boosted valuation to $5B in 2025. Clio's payments business processes billions of dollars annually, and the company recently completed a $1B acquisition of vLex, adding access to over one billion legal documents.
