Overview

Webflow is a no-code website builder that enables designers and developers to create professional websites visually. Founded in 2013 by Vlad Magdalin, the company raised $335M at $4B valuation in March 2022. Revenue reached $213M in 2024, up from $128M in 2023. Webflow powers millions of websites and has become a leader in the no-code movement. The company has stated they have no plans for an IPO, focusing instead on long-term product development. Analysts project potential $7-10B valuation if growth continues.
