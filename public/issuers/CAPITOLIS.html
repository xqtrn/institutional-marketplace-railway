Overview

Capitolis is a pioneering fintech platform that drives financial resource optimization for capital markets. Founded in 2017 by Gil Mandelzis, Tom Glocer, and Igor Teleshevsky, the Israeli startup reached unicorn status in 2022 with a $1.6 billion valuation after raising $110 million in Series D funding. The company's SaaS platform has eliminated trillions of dollars in overall positions for more than 100 financial institutions, including many of the world's largest global and regional banks. In 2024, Citi, Morgan Stanley, State Street, and UBS each invested $5 million and joined the board, validating Capitolis's position as critical capital markets infrastructure. Recognized by CNBC, Euromoney, and Fast Company, the company continues to expand its network and launch new products to optimize how the capital markets operate.
