Overview

DistroKid is a music distribution unicorn that enables independent musicians to upload and distribute their music to major streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, and TikTok. Founded in 2013 by Philip Kaplan (creator of FuckedCompany.com), the San Francisco-based company has revolutionized music distribution with its simple, affordable pricing model that lets artists keep 100% of their royalties. DistroKid reached a $1.3 billion valuation in 2021 and has grown to over 2 million artists with nearly $100 million in annual revenue by 2024. The platform processes over 3 million uploads per month and has paid out billions in royalties to independent artists. Unlike traditional distributors that charge per release, DistroKid charges a flat annual fee, making it the go-to platform for emerging musicians.
