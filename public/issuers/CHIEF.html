Overview

Chief is the largest private network designed to help women rise into positions of power, now serving over 20,000 senior executives with representation from 77% of Fortune 100 companies and over 10,000 organizations. Founded in 2019 by Carolyn Childers and Lindsay Kaplan, Chief provides executive coaching, peer advisory groups, and access to a powerful network of women leaders. With over 40% of members holding C-suite positions, Chief has been recognized as one of TIME's 100 Most Influential Companies and Fast Company's Most Innovative Companies, transforming the landscape of women's leadership.
