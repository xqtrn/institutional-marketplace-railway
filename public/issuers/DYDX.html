Overview

dYdX is a leading decentralized cryptocurrency exchange specializing in perpetual trading, built by founder Antonio Juliano (ex-Coinbase, ex-Uber). Originally launched on Ethereum, dYdX migrated to its own Layer 1 blockchain (dYdX Chain) powered by Cosmos SDK to achieve higher throughput and full decentralization. The platform offers advanced trading features including 20x leverage on perpetual contracts, professional-grade order books, and sub-second trade execution. Backed by top-tier crypto investors including Paradigm, a16z, and Polychain Capital, dYdX has processed billions in trading volume and pioneered the decentralized derivatives market with its DYDX governance token.
