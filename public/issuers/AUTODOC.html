Overview

Autodoc is the leading digital pure-play automotive parts platform in Europe, offering over 6.7 million products from 2,500 brand manufacturers to both consumers and professional workshops across 27 countries. Founded in Berlin in 2008 by Alexej Erdle, Max Wegner, and Vitalij Kungel, the company has grown from a small startup to a â‚¬1.6 billion revenue business with approximately 5,000 employees across 13 locations. Autodoc's e-commerce platform provides vehicle spare parts, accessories, tires, tools, oils, and consumables for cars, trucks, and motorcycles, with its largest logistics center in Szczecin, Poland handling fulfillment across the continent.
