Overview

6sense is the leading account-based marketing (ABM) and revenue intelligence platform, founded in 2013 by Viral Bajaria, Premal Shah, Amanda Kahlow, and Shane Moriah. The platform uses AI to identify anonymous buying signals and predict which accounts are in-market. Raised $526M at $5.2B valuation in 2022 Series E led by Blue Owl and MSD Partners. ARR exceeded $250M by 2024. Named leader in Forrester Wave B2B Revenue Marketing Platforms Q3 2024. Chris Ball became CEO in September 2025, with former CEO Jason Zintak continuing as Chairman.
