Overview

24M Technologies, founded at MIT, is transforming battery manufacturing with its patented SemiSolid electrode technology. The company's chemistry-agnostic platform reduces manufacturing costs by 40% while improving safety, performance, and recyclability. Raised $87M Series H in September 2024 at $1.3B valuation led by Nuovo+. Total funding has reached $526M. Opened a 100 MWh pilot manufacturing facility in Thailand and has licensees including Kyocera and ASAHI Kasei.
