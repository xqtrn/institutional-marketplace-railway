Overview

CoinSwitch Kuber is one of India's largest cryptocurrency exchanges, founded in 2017 by Ashish Singhal, Govind Soni, and Vimal Sagar Tiwari. The platform makes it easy for Indian users to buy, sell, and trade over 100 cryptocurrencies using Indian Rupees with a simple mobile-first interface. CoinSwitch became India's second crypto unicorn in 2021 after raising $260M at a $1.9B valuation led by Andreessen Horowitz (their first India investment), Coinbase Ventures, and Tiger Global. Despite regulatory challenges including India's 30% crypto tax and TDS requirements, CoinSwitch has grown to over 20 million users and continues to be a leading platform for retail crypto adoption in India.
