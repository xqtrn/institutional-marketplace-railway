Overview

Apna is India's largest jobs and professional networking platform, founded in 2019 by Stanford MBA Nirmit Parikh (ex-Apple, Intel). The company became India's fastest unicorn in 2021 after raising $100M Series C led by Tiger Global at a $1.1B valuation. With $190M+ raised from Tiger Global, Insight Partners, Sequoia India, and Lightspeed India, Apna connects the rising workforce with opportunities. In 2024, Kartik Narayan (ex-TeamLease) joined as CEO of Jobs Marketplace, while founder Nirmit Parikh serves as Group CEO.
