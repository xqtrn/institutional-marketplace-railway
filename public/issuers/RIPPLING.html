Overview

Rippling is a unified workforce management platform founded in 2016 by Parker Conrad (former Zenefits CEO). The company provides HR, IT, and finance tools in a single platform, managing everything from onboarding to payroll to device management. In May 2025, Rippling raised $450M at $16.8B valuation (up from $13.5B in April 2024), with $200M allocated for employee share buybacks. Revenue is growing 30%+ annually with $350M+ ARR. Conrad's net worth reached $2.3B. Y Combinator is among Rippling's customers.
