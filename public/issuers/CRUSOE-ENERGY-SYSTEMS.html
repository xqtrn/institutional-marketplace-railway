Overview

Crusoe Energy is an energy-first AI infrastructure company founded in 2018, pioneering Digital Flare Mitigation technology that converts stranded natural gas into power for data centers. By capturing gas that would otherwise be flared or vented, Crusoe reduces methane emissions while providing clean computing infrastructure. The company operates 86 mobile data centers across 30 sites in U.S. oil fields plus Argentina, processing over 10 million cubic feet of gas daily. Crusoe's Abilene, Texas facility is a 998,000 sq ft data center developed through a $3.4B joint venture capable of housing 100,000 GPUs with 1.2GW capacity. Key customers include Sony, Databricks, Together AI, and Luma AI, with the company participating in OpenAI's $500B Stargate project.
