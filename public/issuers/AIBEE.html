Overview

Aibee is a Beijing-based AI unicorn providing enterprise solutions for offline industries including retail, airports, and cultural tourism. Founded in 2017 by Dr. Yuanqing Lin (former head of Baidu Research Institute), the company integrates computer vision, speech recognition, NLP, and big data analytics to transform physical spaces into intelligent environments. Aibee's flagship products include AI-Bee Traffic for customer flow analytics, AI-Bee Mall for comprehensive retail management, and Smart Airport solutions developed in partnership with Huawei. Backed by Xiaomi, Sequoia Capital China, K11, and China Merchants Capital, Aibee reached unicorn status in 2022 and is recognized as a top AI company in China's retail sector. The company serves major clients including China Merchants Group and works across shopping malls, banking, parking, and tourism verticals.
