Overview

Fanatics, Inc. is an American sports merchandise and technology company founded by Michael Rubin in 1995. The company has evolved from a licensed apparel retailer into a diversified digital sports platform spanning Commerce (77% of revenue), Collectibles/Trading Cards (20%), and Sports Betting (4%). In 2024, Fanatics generated $8.1 billion in revenue (up 15% YoY) and expects to be EBITDA profitable in 2025. Fanatics Collectibles has shown remarkable growth, with revenue jumping 40% to $1.6 billion after acquiring Topps in 2022. The company's valuation ranges from $17-31 billion on secondary markets, down from its $31 billion peak in 2022.
