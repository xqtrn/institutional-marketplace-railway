Overview

Aviatrix is a leading cloud network security company that provides enterprises with secure, scalable multicloud connectivity across AWS, Azure, GCP, and hybrid environments. Founded in 2013 by Pankaj Manglik, the company enables network teams to build, scale, and secure cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade visibility and control. Under CEO Doug Merritt, former CEO of Splunk who grew that company from $100M to nearly $3B in ARR, Aviatrix has expanded its platform to address the $30 billion cloud networking market. With 400+ enterprise customers and partnerships with Megaport, Equinix, and major cloud providers, Aviatrix is transforming how organizations connect and secure their multicloud architectures.
