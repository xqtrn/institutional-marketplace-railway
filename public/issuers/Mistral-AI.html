Overview

Mistral AI is a French artificial intelligence startup founded in April 2023 by three former Google DeepMind and Meta researchers. The company specializes in developing open-weight large language models and has rapidly emerged as Europe's leading AI champion. With its flagship chatbot Le Chat and cutting-edge models like Mistral Large and Mixtral, the company has achieved a $14 billion valuation in just over two years. Mistral AI focuses on making frontier AI accessible, transparent, and aligned with European values while competing globally with Silicon Valley giants.
