Overview

Checkr is the leading AI-powered background check platform, enabling companies to hire faster and more fairly at scale. Founded in 2014 and based in San Francisco, Checkr has transformed the employment screening industry with its modern API-first approach and machine learning capabilities. The platform serves over 100,000 customers including gig economy leaders Uber, Instacart, and DoorDash, as well as enterprises across industries. Checkr's technology accelerates background checks while promoting fair chance hiring, helping people with criminal records find employment. Through its nonprofit arm Checkr.org, the company advocates for policies that give all job seekers a fair opportunity.
