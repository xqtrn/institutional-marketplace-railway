Overview

Aledade is the nation's largest network of independent value-based primary care, co-founded in 2014 by Dr. Farzad Mostashari (former National Coordinator for Health IT) and Mat Kendall. The company partners with independent primary care practices and community health centers to form Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs), empowering clinicians with AI-enhanced tools and analytics. Aledade supports 20,000+ clinicians across 2,400+ practices in 46 states, serving nearly 3 million patients. Generated over $1 billion in Medicare savings in 2024 and over $3 billion total since founding.
