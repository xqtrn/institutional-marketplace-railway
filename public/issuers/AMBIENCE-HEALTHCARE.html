Overview

Ambience Healthcare is an AI-powered ambient medical scribe platform founded in 2020. The company's AutoScribe technology eliminates the need for human scribes by automatically documenting patient visits in real-time, reducing clinician charting time by an average of 45%. Ambience achieved unicorn status in 2025 with a $243M Series C led by Oak HC/FT and a16z, reaching $1.25B valuation. More than 40 U.S. health systems have deployed Ambience, including Cleveland Clinic, UCSF Health, Houston Methodist, and Memorial Hermann. The platform integrates with Epic through its Toolbox program. Ambience is competing in the ambient AI documentation space alongside Abridge, with the sector raising nearly $1 billion in 2025 alone.
