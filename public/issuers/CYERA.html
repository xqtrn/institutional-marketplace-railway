Overview

Cyera is a data security company that has become one of the fastest-growing cybersecurity unicorns, tripling its valuation multiple times in 2024. Founded in 2021 by Yotam Segev and Tamar Bar-Ilan, the company provides the foundation for Data Security Posture Management (DSPM), offering agentless discovery, classification, assessment, and protection of structured and unstructured data across clouds, SaaS, data lakes, and on-premises environments. Cyera takes the guesswork out of cloud data security by delivering instant Data Reality to organizations. With offices in New York and Tel Aviv, the company serves enterprise customers globally and is on track to reach $100M ARR.
