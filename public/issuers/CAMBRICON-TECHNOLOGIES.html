Overview

Cambricon Technologies is China's leading AI chip designer, often called 'China's Nvidia' for its focus on AI accelerators and GPGPUs. The partially state-owned company was China's top-performing stock of 2024, surging 383% to reach a $41 billion market cap - outpacing even Nvidia and TSMC in percentage gains. Listed on the Shanghai STAR Market in 2020, Cambricon achieved its first quarterly profit in Q4 2024, projecting 70% revenue growth to 1.2 billion yuan. The company's 7-nanometer AI chips have positioned it as China's domestic answer to US chip restrictions, benefiting from government pressure on Chinese companies to adopt local alternatives to Nvidia processors. In 2024, Cambricon's stock breached the 1,000 yuan mark, a psychological threshold matched only by Kweichow Moutai in A-share history.
