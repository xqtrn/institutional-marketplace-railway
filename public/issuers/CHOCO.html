Overview

Choco is a Berlin-based food ordering platform transforming the supply chain by connecting restaurants with their suppliers through AI-powered technology. Founded in 2018, the company achieved unicorn status in 2022 with a $1.2 billion valuation after raising $310 million in total funding. Choco's platform digitizes the traditionally phone-and-fax-based ordering process, using AI-driven order management to reduce food waste, optimize inventory, and create a more sustainable food system. The company has partnered with major foodservice distributors including UniPro and Dot Foods, and expanded through the acquisition of GOOT in January 2025.
