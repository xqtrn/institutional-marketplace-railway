Overview

BetterUp is the world's largest mental health and coaching platform, serving 600+ organizations with 3,000+ coaches across 70 countries. Founded in 2013 by Alexi Robichaux and Eduardo Medina, BetterUp reached $4.7B valuation in 2021 and generated $215M revenue in 2024. The platform combines human coaching with AI to deliver personalized development powered by 17 million data points - the largest human behavior dataset on performance at work. Notable clients include Chipotle, Chevron, Twilio, Google, and the US Air Force ($99M contract).
