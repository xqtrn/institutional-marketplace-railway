Overview

DealShare is India's second social commerce unicorn after Meesho, offering an online platform for multi-category consumer products using community-driven buying to provide affordable essentials to mass consumers in tier 2 and 3 cities. Founded in 2018 by Vineet Rao, Rajat Shikhar, Sankar Bora, and Sourjyendu Medda, the company achieved unicorn status in January 2022 with $165 million funding led by Tiger Global. The company raised a total of $387 million at a peak valuation of $1.7 billion.
