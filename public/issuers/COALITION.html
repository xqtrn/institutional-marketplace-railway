Overview

Coalition is one of the largest cyber insurance and security providers in the United States and Canada, combining active risk management with comprehensive insurance coverage. Founded in 2017 by Joshua Motta and John Hering, Coalition uses technology to help businesses prevent, prepare for, and respond to cyber incidents. The company reached a $5 billion valuation after raising $800 million in funding and protects over 85,000 organizations with its Active Insurance approach. Coalition's platform provides free cybersecurity tools to policyholders including continuous security monitoring, vulnerability alerts, and incident response support.
