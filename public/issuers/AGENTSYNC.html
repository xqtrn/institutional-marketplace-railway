Overview

AgentSync is a $1.2B InsurTech unicorn founded in 2018 by husband-wife team Niji Sabharwal and Jenn Knight, both former LinkedIn engineers. The company automates insurance producer licensing, compliance, and onboarding workflows for carriers, agencies, and MGAs. AgentSync's platform simplifies the complex regulatory landscape across all 50 states, tracking 302 compliance changes in 2024 alone. David Samuels was appointed CEO in October 2025 to lead the next phase of growth. The company has raised $161M total funding and is recognized as a Forbes Cloud 100 Rising Star and Insurtech Insights Future 50 winner. Headquartered in Denver, AgentSync was named one of Denver's Best Places to Work.
