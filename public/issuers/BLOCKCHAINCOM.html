Overview

Blockchain.com is a cryptocurrency financial services company founded in 2011 by Peter Smith and Nicolas Cary. The company provides a cryptocurrency wallet, exchange, explorer, and institutional services. Blockchain.com reached a peak valuation of $14B in March 2022 after a $300M Series D. In February 2024, the company raised $110M in Series E strategic financing led by Kingsway Capital. The platform has over 90 million wallets created and serves users in 200+ countries. CEO Peter Smith aims to expand trading platforms and institutional services globally.
