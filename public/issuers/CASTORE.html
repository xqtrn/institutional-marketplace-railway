Overview

Castore is a British premium sportswear brand founded in 2015 by brothers Tom and Phil Beahon. The company develops high-performance athletic apparel using patented fabrics and advanced technical engineering, with partnerships including McLaren F1, Newcastle United, Rangers FC, Everton FC, and England Cricket. Tennis champion Andy Murray is both a shareholder and brand ambassador through the AMC collaboration.
