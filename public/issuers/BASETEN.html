Overview

Baseten is the premier AI inference platform powering production workloads for companies building AI applications. Founded in 2019 by Tuhin Srivastava, Philip Howes, Amir Haghighat, and Pankaj Gupta, Baseten provides infrastructure that makes model deployment scalable, affordable, and reliable. The company's revenue grew 10x year-over-year, serving major customers including Abridge, Captions, Clay, OpenEvidence, Patreon, and Writer. Baseten achieved unicorn status in 2025 with a $2.15B valuation after raising $150M in Series D. The company has strategic partnerships with AWS and Google Cloud, and recently acquired Parsed to add reinforcement learning and post-training capabilities.
