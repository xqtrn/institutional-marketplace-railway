Overview

Arqiva is the UK's leading provider of broadcast, media, and smart utility infrastructure. As the sole operator of digital terrestrial television and radio broadcast infrastructure in the United Kingdom, the company connects millions of households to free-to-air TV and radio services. Arqiva operates a network of transmission sites across the UK delivering Freeview, commercial radio, and public service broadcasting. The company has expanded into smart utilities, deploying connected metering infrastructure supporting the UK's transition to smart energy systems.
