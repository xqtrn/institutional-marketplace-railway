Overview

Cedar is the leading patient payment and financial engagement platform for hospitals, health systems, and medical groups. Founded in 2016 by Florian Otto and Arel Lidow, Cedar combines data science and design to create personalized, consumer-friendly billing experiences that improve patient satisfaction and provider collections. The platform has revolutionized medical billing for nearly 50 million patients. Cedar reached unicorn status in 2021 with a $200 million Series D led by Tiger Global at a $3.2 billion valuation. In 2024, Cedar expanded into dental and orthodontics markets and launched the Affordability Navigator to connect patients with financial assistance. TIME recognized Cedar as a World's Top HealthTech Company for 2025.
