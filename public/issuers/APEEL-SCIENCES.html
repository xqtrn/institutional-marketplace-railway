Overview

Apeel Sciences is a food technology company that developed an edible, plant-based coating to extend produce shelf life by 2-3x. Founded in 2012 by James Rogers with a Gates Foundation grant, Apeel tackles the global food waste crisis. With a $2B valuation and $810M raised from Temasek, GIC, Andreessen Horowitz, and celebrity investors like Katy Perry and Oprah Winfrey, Apeel's technology is now available on avocados, citrus, and other produce at major retailers worldwide. In February 2024, Luiz Beling (ex-Bayer, FBN) became CEO.
