Overview

1047 Games is an American video game developer founded in 2017 by Stanford students Ian Proulx and Nicholas Bagamian in their dorm room. The company is best known for Splitgate, a free-to-play multiplayer first-person shooter combining portal mechanics with competitive arena gameplay. After viral success in 2021 with 13M+ downloads, 1047 Games raised $130M at a $1.5B valuation through three funding rounds in just 4 months. After putting Splitgate into maintenance mode to build a scalable sequel, the company pivoted from Splitgate 2 to Splitgate: Arena Reloadedâ€”a back-to-basics relaunch scheduled for December 17, 2025 that removes factions and abilities to focus on classic arena combat. Led by CEO Ian Proulx and featuring Josh Watson (former Rocket League game director) as design director.
