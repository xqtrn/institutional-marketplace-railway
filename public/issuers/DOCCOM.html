Overview

Doc.com is a digital healthcare platform that delivers a tech-powered ecosystem connecting every essential aspect of patient well-being into a seamless, data-driven network. The platform combines AI-optimized telemedicine with instant access to licensed medical professionals, smart pharmacies with AI-driven logistics for medication delivery, and connected clinics that integrate virtual and in-person care. Led by CEO Charles Nader since 2021, the company received a major $300 million investment from Silver Rock Group in February 2024. Doc.com operates across multiple markets including the Americas, leveraging artificial intelligence to improve healthcare accessibility, reduce costs, and enhance patient outcomes across the care continuum.
