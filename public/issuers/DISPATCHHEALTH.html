Overview

DispatchHealth is a Denver-based healthcare unicorn that delivers high-acuity medical care directly to patients in their homes. Founded in 2013 by Dr. Mark Prather and Kevin Riddleberger, the company has built the largest system of in-home medical care in the United States. DispatchHealth's model bridges the gap between traditional urgent care and emergency rooms by sending fully equipped medical teams to patients' homes within hours of a request. The company's technology-enabled platform coordinates care across its network, handling everything from urgent care visits to complex hospital-level care at home. With over $907 million in funding, DispatchHealth partners with major health systems and payers including Humana, Blue Shield, and Optum to reduce emergency room utilization while improving patient outcomes and experience.
