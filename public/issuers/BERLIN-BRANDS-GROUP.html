Overview

Berlin Brands Group (BBG) is a global e-commerce platform that creates, builds, buys, and scales direct-to-consumer brands. Founded in 2005 by Peter Chaljawski, BBG operates over 70 webshops across 28 European countries, with a significant presence on marketplaces like Amazon. The company specializes in acquiring and scaling consumer brands, backed by $800M in funding including a $700M round led by Bain Capital in 2021 that valued the company at over $1 billion. In March 2024, BBG was acquired by HPS Investment Partners.
