Overview

COSMOPlat is Haier's world-leading industrial internet platform, enabling mass customization and intelligent manufacturing transformation across multiple industries. Born from Haier's proprietary RenDanHeYi management philosophy, COSMOPlat connects users directly with manufacturing, allowing real-time customization from design to delivery. The platform has raised $318M at a $7.9B valuation, setting records as the largest Industrial Internet Series A in China. COSMOPlat empowers thousands of enterprises with digital transformation capabilities including digital twin technology, supply chain optimization, and smart factory solutions. The company is preparing for a potential $500M IPO with CICC and HSBC as advisors.
