Overview

Constellation Automotive Group is the UK's largest automotive marketplace, operating three market-leading brands: BCA (Europe's largest used vehicle auction), cinch (online used car retail platform), and WeBuyAnyCar (instant car buying service). Formerly BCA Marketplace on the FTSE 250, the company was taken private by TDR Capital in 2019 and raised Â£1.4B+ in 2021 from sovereign wealth funds including ADIA and GIC, plus Neuberger Berman and Soros Fund Management. The group acquired assets from Cazoo following its 2024 collapse and continues to dominate the UK used car ecosystem.
