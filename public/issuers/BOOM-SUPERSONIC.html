Overview

Boom Supersonic is developing Overture, the world's fastest and most sustainable commercial airliner designed to fly at Mach 1.7 with net-zero carbon emissions. Founded in 2014 by Blake Scholl, the company achieved a major milestone in January 2025 when XB-1, its demonstrator aircraft, became the first privately funded aircraft to break the sound barrier. Boom has 130 orders from American Airlines, United Airlines, and Japan Airlines. The company has raised over $700M and is valued at $3.8B, with a partnership with Northrop Grumman for military variants.
