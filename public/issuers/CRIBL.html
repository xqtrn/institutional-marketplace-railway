Overview

Cribl is the data engine for IT and security, founded in 2018 by former Splunk employees including CEO Clint Sharp. The company pioneered the observability pipeline category, enabling enterprises to route, reduce, and enrich machine data before sending it to any destination. Cribl became the fourth-fastest infrastructure company to reach $100M ARR (centaur status) in under four years, with 163% ARR CAGR over four years. A quarter of Fortune 500 companies are Cribl customers, using its platform to unlock value from observability data while reducing costs. The company's vendor-neutral approach allows enterprises to break free from data lock-in and optimize their security and IT operations data infrastructure.
