Overview

Aleo is the first blockchain enabling private and compliant decentralized applications through zero-knowledge cryptography. The platform allows developers to build applications that verify transactions without revealing sensitive data. Aleo raised $200M in Series B (2022) led by Kora Management and SoftBank Vision Fund 2, valued at $1.45B. This was the largest funding round in zero-knowledge history at the time. An additional $70M Series B+ followed in August 2022. Aleo achieved mainnet launch in 2024, enabling privacy-preserving smart contract execution. Unlike Ethereum where transactions are re-executed by all nodes, Aleo processes transactions off-chain and only verifies proofs on-chain, providing both privacy and scalability.
