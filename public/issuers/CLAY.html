Overview

Clay is the leading go-to-market (GTM) development environment that combines data enrichment, workflow automation, and AI agents to help companies find, contact, and close customers more effectively. The platform aggregates over 130 data sources including first-party, intent, and third-party data in one unified interface. Clay's customers include industry leaders like OpenAI, Canva, Anthropic, Ramp, and Rippling. After 10x-ing revenue in both 2022 and 2023, and 6x-ing in 2024, Clay has become the fastest-growing company in the sales automation space, recently raising $100M at a $3.1 billion valuation.
