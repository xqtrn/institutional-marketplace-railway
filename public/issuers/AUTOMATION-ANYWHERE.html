Overview

Automation Anywhere is a global leader in AI-powered robotic process automation (RPA), enabling enterprises to automate complex business processes using intelligent software bots. Founded in 2003 by Mihir Shukla and Ankur Kothari, the company's cloud-native platform integrates RPA with artificial intelligence, machine learning, and analytics to automate end-to-end workflows across finance, HR, operations, and customer service. With over 1,500 live enterprise deployments and recognition as a Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader for seven consecutive years, Automation Anywhere has become the standard for intelligent automation, helping organizations achieve 25% faster order processing and dramatically improved operational efficiency.
