Overview

Bank of London is a British clearing bank that launched in November 2021 as the United Kingdom's sixth principal clearing bank - only the second new principal clearing bank authorized in the UK in over 250 years. The bank operates an API-based payments and clearing platform and participates in UK Faster Payments and Bacs systems. Founded by Anthony Watson CBE after four years of proprietary technology development, the bank became the first pre-revenue bank to achieve unicorn status. The platform offers cloud-based core banking technology and SaaS delivery for embedded payments, enabling businesses to white-label banking infrastructure via API-first architecture.
