Overview

Copado is the leading DevOps platform for Salesforce and enterprise SaaS applications, serving over 1,500 customers with AI-enabled CI/CD, automated testing, and compliance solutions. Founded in 2013 in Madrid, Spain, the company pioneered Salesforce-native DevOps and has expanded to support multi-cloud enterprise environments. Copado reached unicorn status in 2021 with $140M Series C funding led by Insight Partners, with strategic backing from Salesforce Ventures, SoftBank Vision Fund 2, and IBM Ventures. The platform helps enterprises accelerate digital transformation while maintaining governance and compliance across their cloud portfolios.
