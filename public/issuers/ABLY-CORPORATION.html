Overview

Ably Corporation is South Korea's first unicorn of 2024 and the largest women's fashion platform. Founded in 2018 by Watcha co-founder Kang Seok-hoon, the AI-powered platform uses proprietary recommendation algorithms to personalize style discovery. Operates Ably (women's fashion), 4910 (men's fashion), and Amood (Japan market).
