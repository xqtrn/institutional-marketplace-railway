Overview

Cult.fit is India's first fitness unicorn, founded in 2016 by Mukesh Bansal (Myntra founder) and Ankit Nagori. The company operates an integrated health and fitness platform encompassing cult.fit (gyms and group workouts), mind.fit (mental health), eat.fit (nutrition), and care.fit (primary healthcare). With nearly 500 gyms across India and a digital platform offering live and on-demand workout classes, Cult.fit has built India's largest fitness ecosystem.
