Overview

Caribou (formerly MotoRefi) is a Denver-based auto fintech unicorn helping consumers refinance car loans to save money on monthly payments. Founded in 2016 by Kevin Bennett, the platform connects car owners with credit unions and community banks offering better interest rates. Caribou helps customers save an average of $100+ per month by simplifying the auto refinancing process. The company reached unicorn status in 2022 after raising $115M Series C led by Goldman Sachs. CEO Simon Goodall (ex-Groupon) took over leadership in September 2023 to expand the auto-loan refinance market presence.
