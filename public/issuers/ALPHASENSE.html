Overview

AlphaSense is an AI-powered market intelligence platform founded in 2011 by Jack Kokko and Raj Neervannan. The company provides enterprise search and analytics for investment research, corporate strategy, and competitive intelligence. AlphaSense counts 88% of the S&P 100 as clients including Amazon, Nvidia, Microsoft, Pfizer, and JPMorgan. The platform grew to over 5,000 customers (up 25% in 2024) and exceeded $500M ARR in 2025. In June 2024, AlphaSense raised $650M at a $4B valuation and acquired rival Tegus for $930M, adding expert call transcripts and private company data. The company has 2,000 employees across 8 global offices.
