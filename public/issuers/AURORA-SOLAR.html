Overview

Aurora Solar is the leading platform for solar sales, design, and permitting, trusted by more than 7,000 solar organizations worldwide including 90% of the top 100 solar installers. Founded in 2013 by Stanford MBA students Christopher Hopper and Samuel Adeyemo, the company has built comprehensive software tools that enable solar professionals to remotely design accurate systems, generate compelling sales proposals, and streamline permitting processes. With over 20 million solar projects designed on its platform and 100,000+ new projects created weekly, Aurora Solar has become the industry standard for residential and commercial solar design.
