Overview

Miro is a visual collaboration platform (online whiteboard) that enables distributed teams to work effectively together. Founded in 2011 by Andrey Khusid in Russia (now HQ in San Francisco), Miro reached $17.5B valuation in January 2022 after raising $400M Series C. The platform has 90M+ users across 250,000+ organizations, with 99% of Fortune 100 as customers. ARR reached $560M in 2023 with 33% YoY growth.
