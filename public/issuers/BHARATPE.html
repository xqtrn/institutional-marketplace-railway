Overview

BharatPe is an Indian fintech company that provides digital payment solutions and financial services to small merchants and grocery stores. Founded in 2018 by Ashneer Grover and Shashvat Nakrani, BharatPe pioneered interoperable QR codes for UPI payments in India. The company serves over 10 million merchants and has raised $604M in funding with a $2.85B valuation. In April 2024, Nalin Negi was elevated to CEO. BharatPe has expanded into merchant lending, POS solutions, and recently acquired Trillion Loans in 2024.
