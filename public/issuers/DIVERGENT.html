Overview

Divergent Technologies is a Los Angeles-based unicorn that has developed the world's first end-to-end software-hardware production system for industrial digital manufacturing. The Divergent Adaptive Production System (DAPS) integrates AI-driven generative design software, novel materials, 3D printing, and automated fixtureless assembly to create complex structures for automotive, aerospace, and defense applications. Founded by Kevin Czinger, the company has raised over $816 million and works with luxury automakers including Aston Martin, Bugatti, and McLaren, as well as defense contractors like Lockheed Martin and Raytheon. The Czinger 21C hypercar, which features over 350 additively manufactured components, showcases the platform's revolutionary capabilities. With over 500 patent filings, Divergent is fundamentally transforming how vehicles and aerospace structures are designed and built.
