Overview

airSlate is a Boston-based Ukrainian-founded unicorn providing no-code business process automation and document management solutions. Founded in 2008 by Vadim Yasinovsky (inventor of the internet spider) as PDFfiller, the company was joined by Borya Shakhnovich in 2012 and rebranded to airSlate in 2018. The company achieved unicorn status in June 2022 with a $51.5M round led by G Squared and UiPath Ventures at a $1.25B valuation. airSlate's product portfolio includes signNow (eSignature), pdfFiller (PDF editing), DocHub, US Legal Forms, and the no-code WorkFlow automation platformâ€”serving over 100 million users and 900,000 customers with $100M+ ARR. In October 2023, airSlate acquired Instapage to add conversion-optimized landing page automation to its suite. Backed by General Catalyst, Morgan Stanley, and HighSage, the company became the third Ukrainian unicorn after GitLab and Grammarly.
