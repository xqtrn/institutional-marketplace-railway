Overview

CHEQ is the global leader in go-to-market security, protecting businesses from fake traffic, bots, and malicious network requests. Trusted by over 15,000 companies including Fortune 50 enterprises, CHEQ's cloud platform monitors 1 million domains and processes 6 trillion signals daily to filter invalid traffic and protect marketing investments. The Tel Aviv-based company has built an incomparable network effect in cybersecurity, enabling companies to ensure genuine human engagement across all digital touchpoints in the AI-driven customer journey.
