Overview

Creditas is one of Latin America's largest fintech platforms, founded in 2012 by Spanish entrepreneur Sergio Furio in SÃ£o Paulo, Brazil. The company pioneered asset-backed consumer lending in Brazil, using homes and automobiles as collateral to offer loans at significantly lower interest rates than traditional unsecured credit. Creditas operates a fully digital platform integrating credit scoring, loan origination, and asset valuation. The company has expanded into auto financing, home equity loans, payroll loans for employees, and a marketplace for selling cars and real estate. Having weathered the 2022-23 tech downturn with significant restructuring, Creditas is now targeting profitability while growing its R$5+ billion credit portfolio.
