Overview

MXC Foundation is a Berlin-based non-profit promoting the global adoption of LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Network) technology through blockchain. Operating one of the world's largest decentralized IoT networks, MXC combines LoRaWAN infrastructure with crypto mining. Their MXProtocol enables users to deploy LPWAN miners and earn MXC tokens for IoT data transactions. Backed by investors including CITIC Group, OKX Ventures, and Huobi. First blockchain-IoT company listed on German stock exchange.
