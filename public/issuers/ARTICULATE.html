Overview

Articulate is the world's leading online training platform, used by more than 106,000 organizations worldwide, including all Fortune 100 companies. Founded in 2002 by Adam Schwartz with his life savings, the company bootstrapped for nearly 20 years before raising a massive $1.5 billion Series A in 2021 at a $3.75 billion valuationâ€”one of the largest Series A rounds ever. Articulate 360 includes Storyline 360 for interactive courses, Rise 360 for responsive web-based learning, and Reach LMS for course delivery. In 2024, the company launched AI Assistant, enabling creators to generate lesson outlines, text, images, quizzes, and speech with AI. With 100+ product updates in 2024 and 1.4 million members in the E-Learning Heroes community, Articulate earned the 2026 TrustRadius Buyer's Choice Award.
