Overview

AngelList is the leading venture infrastructure platform, founded in 2010 by Naval Ravikant and Babak Nivi. The platform powers syndicates, rolling funds, and venture operations for founders and investors. With a $4.1B valuation, AngelList participates in 28% of all venture deals backed by top-tier funds. The company comprises AngelList Venture, AngelList Talent, and AngelList India. Naval Ravikant's angel portfolio includes early investments in Twitter, Uber, Postmates, and Yammer.
