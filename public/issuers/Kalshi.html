Overview

Kalshi is America's first federally regulated prediction market exchange, founded in 2018 by Tarek Mansour and Luana Lopes Lara. The platform allows users to trade event contracts on real-world outcomes, from political elections and economic indicators to sports, weather, and cultural events. After an 18-month regulatory journey, Kalshi received designation as a contract market from the CFTC in November 2020 and launched its trading platform in July 2021. The company made headlines in October 2024 when a federal appeals court ruled in its favor, allowing it to offer political prediction marketsâ€”the first fully regulated election markets in the U.S. As of October 2025, Kalshi has achieved a $5 billion valuation following a $300 million Series D funding round and now operates in over 140 countries. With 2 million users and $50 billion in projected annualized trading volume for 2025, Kalshi is transforming how people hedge risk and capitalize on their knowledge of world events.
