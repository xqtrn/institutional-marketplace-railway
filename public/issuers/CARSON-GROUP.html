Overview

Carson Group is one of America's largest independent wealth management firms, founded in 1983 by Ron Carson in Omaha, Nebraska. The company operates three core businesses: Carson Wealth (wealth management), Carson Coaching (advisor coaching and consulting), and Carson Partners (RIA network with 110+ partner offices). Bain Capital acquired a 29% stake in 2021, valuing the firm at over $1 billion. The company serves 50,000+ families with $35.5 billion in assets under management. In 2024, founder Ron Carson stepped down as CEO, with Burt White assuming the role while Carson remains Chairman.
