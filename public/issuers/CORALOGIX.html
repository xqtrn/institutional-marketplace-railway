Overview

Coralogix is a full-stack observability platform powered by AI, helping over 4,000 enterprises monitor, troubleshoot, and secure their digital infrastructure. The platform combines logs, metrics, traces, and security in a unified solution with real-time streaming analytics and machine learning-driven insights. Founded in Israel and reaching unicorn status in June 2025 with $115M Series E funding, Coralogix has achieved 7x revenue growth since 2022. The company acquired AI startup Aporia in December 2024 to enhance its AI capabilities and launched 'Olly,' an agentic AI assistant for automated observability workflows.
