Overview

bolttech is a leading global insurtech unicorn specializing in embedded insuranceâ€”providing protection products integrated into customer purchase experiences. Founded in 2020 by insurance veteran Rob Schimek, the company achieved unicorn status in 2021 and has raised over $247 million in Series C funding at a $2.1 billion valuation. bolttech connects approximately 700 distribution partners with more than 230 insurers globally across 30 markets in North America, Asia, and Europe. The platform processes approximately $65 billion in annualized quoted premiums. Major partners include Allianz, Apple, AXA, Liberty Mutual, Samsung, Lazada, and Home Credit. In December 2025, bolttech acquired Kenya's mTek in a notable East African insurtech exit. Strategic investors include Tokio Marine, MetLife, Sumitomo Corporation, Generali, and Dragon Fund.
