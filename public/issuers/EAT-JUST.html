Overview

Eat Just is a pioneering food technology company producing both plant-based foods and cultivated meat. The company became a unicorn in 2016 and has been making moves toward an IPO with a target valuation of $3 billion. Through its GOOD Meat subsidiary, Eat Just became one of the first companies to receive both FDA and USDA approval for cultivated chicken in 2023. GOOD Meat has raised $267 million, making it the most funded cell-cultured meat company to date. The company's flagship products include JUST Egg plant-based egg substitute and GOOD Meat cultivated chicken, sold in Singapore and approved for sale in the United States.
