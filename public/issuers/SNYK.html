Overview

Snyk is a developer-first security platform founded in 2015 that helps developers find and fix vulnerabilities in code, dependencies, containers, and infrastructure as code. The company pioneered the shift-left security movement. Snyk reached $300M ARR in October 2024 with 5,000+ enterprise customers and is targeting cash-flow positive in 2025. The company's AI-native SAST product (Snyk Code) alone generates $100M ARR. While Snyk reached an $8.5B valuation in 2021, recent valuations have compressed. In November 2024, Snyk acquired Probely for dynamic application security testing.
