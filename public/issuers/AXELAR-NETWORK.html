Overview

Axelar Network is the leading blockchain interoperability platform connecting 68+ chains and enabling seamless cross-chain communication. Created by the founding team behind Algorand blockchain, Axelar provides the infrastructure for institutions like JP Morgan, Deutsche Bank, Apollo, and Mastercard to tokenize real-world assets across multiple blockchains. The network powers over $10 billion in cumulative transaction volume with 71% growth in active users in 2024. As part of Singapore's Project Guardian, Axelar collaborates with major financial institutions on asset tokenization. The platform integrates with Sui, TON, Solana, and dozens of EVM and Cosmos chains through its Mobius Development Stack.
