Overview

Cabify is Spain's first unicorn and a leading ride-hailing platform operating across Spain and Latin America. Founded in 2011 by Juan de Antonio, the company reached unicorn status in 2018 after raising $160 million at a $1.4 billion valuation. Cabify provides multi-modal urban transportation services including ride-hailing, delivery, and corporate mobility solutions across more than 40 cities. The company has achieved three consecutive years of over 30% growth and reached break-even on EBITDA globally. With over 50 million registered users and 1.5 million driver partners, Cabify is committed to having 100% zero-emission trips by 2025 in Spain and 2030 in Latin America, positioning itself as a leader in sustainable urban mobility.
