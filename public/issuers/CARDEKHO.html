Overview

CarDekho is India's largest automotive digital platform and Rajasthan's first unicorn startup, founded in Jaipur in 2008 by brothers Amit and Anurag Jain. The company operates a comprehensive ecosystem covering new car research, used car transactions, auto insurance, and auto financing. CarDekho Group includes BikeDekho (two-wheelers), InsuranceDekho (insurance distribution), OTO (Indonesia), and Zigwheels (Southeast Asia). CEO Amit Jain is known as a Shark Tank India judge. The company is planning an IPO on Indian exchanges valued at approximately $2-2.5 billion.
