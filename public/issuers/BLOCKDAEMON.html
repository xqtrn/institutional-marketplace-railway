Overview

Blockdaemon is the leading institutional blockchain infrastructure platform, securing over $110 billion in digital assets for more than 400 institutions including exchanges, custodians, and financial enterprises. Founded in 2017, the company has raised $468 million in total funding at a $3.25 billion valuation as of January 2022, with investors including Goldman Sachs, Citi Ventures, and Salesforce Ventures. Blockdaemon is the #1 staking provider for institutions with $10 billion in staked assets across 50+ blockchain protocols, delivering 99.9% uptime and managing 250,000 nodes globally. In 2025, Blockdaemon won the Best DeFi Infrastructure Platform award, launched the Earn Stack for institutional staking and DeFi, and acquired expand.network for unified DeFi API capabilities. The company is reportedly eyeing a 2026 IPO.
