Overview

Crexi is a leading commercial real estate technology platform founded in 2015, transforming how CRE professionals market, analyze, and transact properties. The platform combines a comprehensive property marketplace with AI-powered analytics, providing brokers, investors, and tenants with tools to accelerate deal cycles. With millions of monthly users, Crexi has facilitated over $615 billion in closed transactions and marketed more than $7 trillion in property value over nine years. The platform offers listing syndication, market intelligence, property research, and transaction management capabilities that streamline the entire commercial real estate workflow.
