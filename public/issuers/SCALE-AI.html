Overview

Scale AI is the leading data infrastructure company powering the development of artificial intelligence. Founded in 2016, Scale provides high-quality training data and AI infrastructure to enterprises, AI labs, and government agencies. With 90% of business now driven by generative AI, Scale has become essential infrastructure for companies training large language models and other AI systems. In June 2025, Meta acquired a 49% stake in Scale for $14.3 billion, valuing the company at over $29 billion. The deal brought founder Alexandr Wang to Meta to lead a new "superintelligence" research lab.
