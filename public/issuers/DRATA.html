Overview

Drata is the leading trust management platform that automates security compliance through continuous control monitoring and evidence collection. Founded in 2020 by Adam Markowitz, Daniel Marashlian, and Troy Markowitz, Drata helps thousands of companies streamline compliance with frameworks including SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS, and NIST guidelines. The platform integrates with over 100 cloud services and applications to automatically collect evidence, monitor security controls, and maintain continuous compliance. Backed by prominent tech leaders including Satya Nadella (Microsoft), Jeff Weiner (LinkedIn), and Frank Slootman (Snowflake), Drata achieved unicorn status at a $2B valuation.
