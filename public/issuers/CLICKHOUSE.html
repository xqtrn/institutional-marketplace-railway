Overview

ClickHouse is the fastest and most resource-efficient open-source columnar database management system for online analytical processing (OLAP). Originally developed at Yandex, ClickHouse enables real-time analytics with SQL queries across petabytes of data. The company commercializes the open-source project with ClickHouse Cloud, serving leading enterprises including Tesla, Sony, Anthropic, Lyft, and Instacart. After raising $350M in Series C at a $6.35 billion valuation, ClickHouse is positioned as a critical infrastructure layer for AI-era analytics.
