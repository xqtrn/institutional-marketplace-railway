Overview

Clipboard Health is a profitable app-based marketplace that matches healthcare professionals with open shifts at healthcare facilities. Founded in 2016, the company has built a platform that enables nurses, CNAs, and other healthcare workers to find available shifts at nearby facilities while helping facilities fill staffing gaps in real-time. The company raised $80 million across Series B and C rounds, achieving unicorn status at a $1.3 billion valuation. Clipboard Health has expanded beyond healthcare into education with Clipboard Education, connecting schools with substitute teachers using the same shift-placement model.
