Overview

Airwallex is a Singapore-headquartered (Melbourne-founded) global payments unicorn providing financial infrastructure for businesses operating internationally. Founded in 2015 by Jack Zhang, Lucy Liu, Max Li, Xijing Dai, and Ki-lok Wong, the company became Australia's fastest unicorn in 2019. In December 2025, Airwallex raised $330M Series G at an $8B valuation led by Addition, T. Rowe Price, and Robinhood, with IPO planned for 2026. The platform surpassed $1B ARR in October 2025 (90% YoY growth) and processes $235B+ in annualized transaction volume for 150,000 businesses worldwide. Airwallex holds 80 licenses across North America, Europe, Middle East, and APAC, powering global accounts in 10+ currencies, payment acceptance in 70+ countries, payouts to 120+ destinations, and Visa corporate cards. In 2025, the company acquired CTIN Pay (Vietnam), entered New Zealand, and established San Francisco as dual global HQ.
