Overview

Avenue One is a technology service platform and investment marketplace that connects institutional investors with local operators for single-family rental (SFR) real estate investing. Founded in 2020, the company leverages artificial intelligence and on-the-ground market insights to enable financial institutions to efficiently purchase and manage rental properties across the United States. Avenue One's capital marketplace has on-boarded over $2 billion in assets, providing institutional portfolio managers unprecedented visibility and control in creating custom portfolios. By not acquiring properties directly, Avenue One operates as a pure technology platform, partnering local experts who find, buy, renovate, lease, and manage properties on behalf of institutional clients.
