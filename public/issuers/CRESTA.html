Overview

Cresta is a contact center AI company founded in 2017 by Stanford AI Lab dropouts Zayd Enam and Tim Shi, along with renowned AI pioneer Sebastian Thrun. The company builds generative AI solutions that help enterprises improve agent performance and automate customer interactions in real-time. Cresta's platform analyzes millions of conversations to identify best practices, coach agents live during calls, and surface insights for managers. Major customers include CarMax, Blue Nile, Intuit, Cox, and Holiday Inn. The company has nearly quadrupled its ARR and doubled its customer base in two years, earning a spot on the Forbes AI 50 List of 2024.
