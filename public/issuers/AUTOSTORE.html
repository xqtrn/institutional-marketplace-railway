Overview

AutoStore is the world's leading cube storage automation system, pioneering a revolutionary approach to warehouse logistics that maximizes storage density while dramatically improving picking efficiency. Founded in 1996 in Norway, the company has deployed over 42,000 robots across 1,000+ installations in 46 countries, commanding 97% of the cubic storage automation market. AutoStore's grid-based system uses compact robots that retrieve bins from a dense cube structure, delivering items to human operators with remarkable speed and accuracy. The company went public on the Oslo Stock Exchange in October 2021 in Norway's largest IPO in two decades, with SoftBank as a major shareholder. Major customers include PUMA, DHL, and leading retailers worldwide.
