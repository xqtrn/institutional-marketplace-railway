Overview

Castelion is a defense technology company founded by former SpaceX executives to build America's first mass-producible hypersonic strike weapons. Headquartered in El Segundo, California, the company is developing Blackbeard, the first U.S. hypersonic system engineered from inception for industrial-rate output and commercial unit cost. In 2025, Castelion conducted more than 20 development flight tests and secured contracts from the U.S. Army and Navy for platform integration. The company is building Project Ranger, a 1,000-acre solid rocket motor manufacturing facility in New Mexico capable of producing thousands of Blackbeard missiles per year. Castelion raised $450 million including a $350 million Series B in December 2025.
