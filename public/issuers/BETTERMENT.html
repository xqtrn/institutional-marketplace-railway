Overview

Betterment is the largest independent digital investment advisor in the United States, serving over 900,000 customers with more than $55 billion in assets under management. Founded in 2008 by Jon Stein and Eli Broverman, and launched at TechCrunch Disrupt NY 2010 where it won 'Biggest New York Disruptor', Betterment pioneered the robo-advisor category. The platform offers automated investing, retirement planning, and cash management services. In 2024, Betterment acquired Goldman Sachs' Marcus Invest unit and announced the acquisition of Ellevest's automated investing business in 2025.
