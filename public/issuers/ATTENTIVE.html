Overview

Attentive is the leading AI-powered mobile messaging platform that drives 20.5% of total online revenue for businesses through personalized SMS and email marketing. Founded in 2016, the company has built the most comprehensive text message marketing solution, serving thousands of brands including major retailers and e-commerce companies. Attentive's platform uses AI and machine learning to optimize message timing, personalization, and campaign performance, helping brands build direct relationships with consumers through their mobile devices.
