Overview

Retool is a low-code platform for building internal tools, founded in 2017 by David Hsu (YC alum). The platform allows developers to build custom internal applications 10x faster using drag-and-drop components connected to any database or API. Retool raised $45M Series C2 at $3.2B valuation in 2022, with investors including Sequoia Capital and Stripe founders John and Patrick Collison. ARR reached $120M in October 2025, up from $90M in 2024. Enterprise customers include Amazon, Mercedes-Benz, Progressive, Peloton, and Rakuten. Recent AI launches (AppGen, Agents) introduced new monetization.
