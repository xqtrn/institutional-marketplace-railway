Overview

Chargebee is the leading subscription billing and revenue management platform, powering end-to-end recurring billing, subscription management, and automated invoicing for over 18,000 SaaS and subscription businesses. Founded in 2011, the company has grown revenue to $202.6 million in 2024, up 63% from the prior year. Chargebee was named a Leader in the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Recurring Billing Applications. The platform helps companies monetize their subscription offerings, handle complex pricing models, manage revenue recognition, and reduce churn through intelligent dunning and retention tools.
