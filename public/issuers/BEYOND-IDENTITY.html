Overview

Beyond Identity is a secure access platform that eliminates identity-based attacks with passwordless, phishing-resistant multi-factor authentication. Co-founded by Jim Clark (Netscape founder) and TJ Jermoluk, the company provides zero-trust identity architecture that assesses 70+ contextual risk factors for authentication. Beyond Identity reached unicorn status with a $1.1 billion valuation after raising $205M across three funding rounds. The platform is trusted by organizations including Snowflake, Cornell University, Charlotte Hornets, and Steve Madden.
