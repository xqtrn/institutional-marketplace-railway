Overview

Dubizzle Group (formerly EMPG - Emerging Markets Property Group) is the leading online classifieds and property portal platform across the Middle East, North Africa, and South Asia. The company operates multiple market-leading brands including Dubizzle, Bayut, Zameen.com, OLX Pakistan, OLX Egypt, OLX Lebanon, and Lamudi. Founded by brothers Imran and Zeeshan Ali Khan, the group achieved unicorn status in 2020 and has since grown to over 2,400 employees across multiple markets. With platforms serving millions of users across property, automotive, and general classifieds, Dubizzle Group is positioning for a Dubai IPO in 2025 with Goldman Sachs, Emirates NBD, HSBC, and Morgan Stanley as mandated banks.
