Overview

Anyscale is the company behind Ray, the open-source distributed computing framework that powers AI workloads at OpenAI, Uber, Netflix, and Spotify. Founded in 2019 by UC Berkeley researchers Robert Nishihara, Philipp Moritz, and Ion Stoica (Databricks co-founder), Anyscale reached unicorn status with $100M Series C in 2021. In July 2024, Aruba Networks founder Keerti Melkote joined as CEO to accelerate enterprise adoption. Ray precisely orchestrates AI infrastructure across any accelerator at any scale, making distributed ML accessible to every team.
