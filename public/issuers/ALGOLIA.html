Overview

Algolia is the AI Retrieval Platform transforming how the world discovers information. Founded in 2012 in Paris by Nicolas Dessaigne and Julien Lemoine, the company provides API-first search and discovery solutions powering over 1.75 trillion searches per year. Algolia's platform combines keyword search, vector-based NeuralSearch, rules, and personalization to deliver instant, relevant results. With 18,000+ customers including Twitch, Stripe, and Under Armour, Algolia is recognized in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for Search and Product Discovery. The company pioneered agentic and generative AI search capabilities in 2025.
