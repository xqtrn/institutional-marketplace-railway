Overview

Neon is a serverless PostgreSQL database platform founded in 2021 by Nikita Shamgunov (ex-SingleStore CEO), Heikki Linnakangas, and Stas Kelvich. The company raised $130M total including $25M from Microsoft's M12 in August 2024. In May 2025, Databricks announced acquisition of Neon for approximately $1B. Neon's serverless architecture features autoscaling and scale-to-zero capabilities. Key investors include M12, General Catalyst, Menlo Ventures, Databricks Ventures, and Founders Fund.
