Overview

Automattic is the company behind WordPress.com, WooCommerce, Tumblr, and a constellation of products that power much of the internet. Founded in 2005 by Matt Mullenweg, co-creator of WordPress, Automattic operates as a fully distributed company with employees across 90+ countries. WordPress powers 43% of all websites globally, while WooCommerce dominates open-source e-commerce. The company has built a unique culture around remote work and open-source principles, acquiring complementary businesses like Tumblr ($3M, 2019), Pocket Casts, Day One, and universal messaging app Beeper ($125M, 2024) to expand its mission of democratizing publishing and communication on the web.
