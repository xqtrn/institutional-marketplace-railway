Overview

Devoted Health is a HealthTech company offering Medicare Advantage plans that combine health insurance coverage with technology-enabled primary care services. Founded by Todd Park (former U.S. CTO) and Ed Park, the company serves seniors with coordinated care, personalized health guidance, and $0 premium plans in select markets. Devoted has achieved 5-Star ratings from CMS in Florida and Ohio and grew membership 60% in 2024 to over 227,000 members. The company operates in 13 states with a focus on delivering better health outcomes through integrated technology, dedicated care guides, and value-based care models.
