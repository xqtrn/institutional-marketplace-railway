Overview

CRED is an Indian fintech unicorn founded in 2018 by Kunal Shah, creator of Freecharge which sold to Axis Bank. The members-only platform rewards creditworthy Indians for paying credit card bills on time, requiring a credit score of 750+ for membership. CRED has expanded beyond bill payments to offer CRED Pay (merchant payments), CRED Cash (short-term credit lines), CRED Mint (investments), CRED Travel, and CRED Garage (premium commerce). The platform serves over 35% of India's credit card users, processing billions in transactions monthly. Known for viral Super Bowl-style advertisements featuring Bollywood legends, CRED has built India's most premium fintech brand targeting the country's top 1% of credit users.
