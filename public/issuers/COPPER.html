Overview

Copper is a leading institutional digital asset infrastructure provider, offering secure custody, trading, and prime services to hedge funds, asset managers, and financial institutions. The company's award-winning MPC (multi-party computation) custody solution eliminates single points of failure with distributed key shards, while ClearLoop enables off-exchange trading without moving assets. Backed by Tiger Global, Barclays, and Dawn Capital with $791M in funding at a $2B valuation, Copper serves clients including Brevan Howard Digital ($25B+ AUM) and 21Shares (world's largest crypto ETP issuer). The company is pursuing US licensing including BitLicense as it expands blockchain-based collateral management into traditional finance.
