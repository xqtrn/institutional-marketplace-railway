Overview

ConcertAI is the leading AI-powered real-world data and analytics company for oncology research, serving over 50 life science companies and 2,000+ healthcare providers globally. Part of the SymphonyAI portfolio, ConcertAI has built the most comprehensive research-grade oncology data network, combining real-world data, advanced analytics, and AI/ML capabilities to accelerate clinical trials, drug development, and precision medicine. The company achieved profitability in 2024 with mid-teens EBITDA margins and 72% recurring revenue, establishing itself as the definitive platform for cancer research intelligence.
