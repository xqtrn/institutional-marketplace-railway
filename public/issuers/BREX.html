Overview

Brex is a corporate spend management platform founded in 2017 by Henrique Dubugras and Pedro Franceschi, two Brazilian entrepreneurs who previously founded Pagar.me (Brazil's Stripe). Brex provides corporate cards, expense management, and financial services for startups and enterprises. The company reached $700M ARR in August 2025, up 50% YoY. Customers include DoorDash, Coinbase, Robinhood, and Roblox. In 2024, Pedro became sole CEO while Henrique moved to Chairman as the company targets a 2025 IPO.
