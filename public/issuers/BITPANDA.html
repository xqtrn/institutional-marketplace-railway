Overview

Bitpanda is Europe's leading cryptocurrency broker and digital asset platform, founded in 2014 by Eric Demuth, Paul Klanschek, and Christian Trummer in Vienna. As Austria's first unicorn with a $4.1B valuation, Bitpanda offers trading in 3,200+ digital assets including crypto, stocks, ETFs, and commodities to over 7 million users.
