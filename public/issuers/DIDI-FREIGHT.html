Overview

DiDi Freight is a Chinese logistics unicorn and the trucking division of DiDi Chuxing, the country's largest ride-hailing company. The platform uses technology to match shippers with truck drivers for on-demand freight transportation, similar to Uber Freight. DiDi Freight competes with Full Truck Alliance (Manbang) in China's massive trucking market, leveraging DiDi's expertise in transportation technology, logistics optimization, and driver management. The company raised $1.5B in its debut funding round at a $3B valuation, backed by Temasek, Yunfeng Capital, and other major investors.
