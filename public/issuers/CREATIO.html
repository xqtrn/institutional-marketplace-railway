Overview

Creatio is a global vendor of one platform to automate industry workflows and CRM with no-code and AI-native technology. Founded in 2014, the company has become the sixth Ukrainian-founded unicorn, joining GitLab, Grammarly, People.ai, Unstoppable Domains, and airSlate. Creatio's platform enables enterprises to build and customize applications without coding, accelerating digital transformation across sales, marketing, and service operations. The platform is recognized as the only leader in Forrester Wave: Low-Code Platforms for Citizen Developers Q1 2024. Headquartered in Boston with an R&D center in Kyiv, Creatio serves thousands of customers across 100 countries including Colgate-Palmolive, AMD, MetLife, OKI, and Saudi Telecom Company.
