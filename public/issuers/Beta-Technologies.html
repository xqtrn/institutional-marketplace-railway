Overview

Beta Technologies is a Vermont-based electric aviation company pioneering the development of all-electric aircraft and charging infrastructure. Founded in 2017 by pilot-engineer Kyle Clark, the company has developed the ALIA family of aircraft including the CX300 conventional takeoff model and A250 eVTOL. In November 2025, Beta went public on the NYSE under ticker BETA, raising over $1 billion at a $7.4 billion valuation. The CX300 received FAA special airworthiness certification in November 2024 and completed the first U.S. passenger-carrying electric flight in June 2025. With an order backlog of nearly 900 aircraft from customers including UPS, Air New Zealand, United Therapeutics, and Republic Airways, Beta is leading the electric aviation revolution. The company has also built 46+ charging sites across the country.
