Overview

Deputy is an Australian workforce management unicorn providing scheduling, time and attendance, and communication tools for hourly and shift-based workers. The platform enables businesses to create optimized schedules, track time via mobile apps, manage compliance, and communicate with distributed teams. Deputy serves over 365,000 workplaces globally across industries including retail, hospitality, healthcare, and manufacturing. The company's AI-powered auto-scheduling and demand forecasting help businesses reduce labor costs while improving employee satisfaction and work-life balance.
