Overview

Cohesity is the world's largest data protection software provider following its December 2024 acquisition of Veritas' enterprise data protection business. Founded in 2013 by Mohit Aron (who also founded Nutanix), Cohesity consolidates backup, disaster recovery, and file services into a unified platform powered by AI. The company's DataProtect and DataHawk solutions help organizations secure and manage their data across multi-cloud environments. With the Veritas combination, Cohesity now serves over 12,000 customers including 86 of the Fortune 100, reaching $1.5B ARR in just 11 years - the fastest in the data protection industry. The company is positioned for a potential IPO in 2025.
