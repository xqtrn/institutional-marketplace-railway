Overview

Anthropic is an AI safety and research company founded in 2021 by former OpenAI executives Dario and Daniela Amodei. It focuses on building reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems, with its flagship product being the Claude family of large language models. The company emphasizes AI alignment with human values and has rapidly grown to become a key player in the AI industry. As of November 2025, Anthropic has achieved an implied valuation of approximately $350 billion following a massive partnership with Microsoft ($5B) and Nvidia ($10B), with $30B committed to Azure compute. The company is on track for $9B ARR by year-end 2025, with Claude Code generating $500M+ in annualized revenue alone. Anthropic projects $20-26B ARR in 2026 and up to $70B by 2028.
