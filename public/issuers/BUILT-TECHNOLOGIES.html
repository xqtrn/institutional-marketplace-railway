Overview

Built Technologies is an enterprise technology company providing cloud-based construction lending software. Founded in 2014 in Nashville by Chase Gilbert, Scott Sohr, and Andrew Sohr, Built digitizes the construction finance ecosystem connecting lenders, builders, and borrowers. The company reached unicorn status in 2021 with a $1.5B valuation after raising $125M Series D led by TCV. Built grew revenue from $14.8M in 2021 to $83M in 2024, serving 150+ customers including major banks and construction lenders.
