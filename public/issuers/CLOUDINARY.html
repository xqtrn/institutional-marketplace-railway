Overview

Cloudinary is a cloud-based media management platform founded in 2012 in Israel by Itai Lahan (CEO), Tal Lev-Ami (CTO), and Nadav Soferman (CPO). The company provides image and video management, transformation, optimization, and delivery via APIs. Cloudinary raised $100M at $2B valuation. Revenue reached $94M in 2024. The company was named to Forbes Cloud 100 for 6 consecutive years. Investors include Blackstone, Salesforce Ventures, and Bessemer Venture Partners.
