Overview

Aura is the leading all-in-one digital safety platform that protects families from online threats, identity theft, and privacy risks. Founded by Hari Ravichandran, the company combines identity theft protection, credit monitoring, VPN services, antivirus, password management, and parental controls into a unified platform. Following a 2024 split that created two independent companies - Aura and Point Wild (formerly Pango Group) - Aura has accelerated growth with $150M ARR and over 40% year-over-year growth, serving families across the United States.
