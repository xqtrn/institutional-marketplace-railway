Overview

DiDi Autonomous Driving (DiDi Woya) is the self-driving technology division of DiDi Chuxing, China's largest ride-hailing platform. The company develops Level 4 autonomous driving systems for robotaxi services, combining hardware, software, and operational capabilities. DiDi's autonomous driving unit has been testing robotaxis in Shanghai and other Chinese cities, leveraging the vast driving data collected from DiDi's ride-hailing network to train and improve its autonomous systems. The division represents DiDi's bet on the future of mobility, aiming to deploy safe, efficient, and affordable autonomous ride-hailing services at scale. In November 2023, Guangzhou Automobile Group (GAC) led a strategic Series C investment in the autonomous driving unit.
