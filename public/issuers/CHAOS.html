Overview

Chaos is a global leader in visualization technology, providing accessible tools that simplify and accelerate workflows for artists, architects, designers, and creative professionals worldwide. The company's flagship product V-Ray has been honored with an Academy Award for Technical Achievement and an Engineering Emmy for its contributions to computer graphics. Following the 2022 merger with Enscape, backed by TA Associates and LEA Partners, Chaos has nearly 800 employees across 11 offices globally. The combined portfolio includes V-Ray, Corona, Enscape, Cylindo, and Anima, serving industries from architectural visualization to Hollywood visual effects.
