Overview

Arctic Wolf is a global leader in security operations, delivering the industry's first cloud-native security operations platform to end cyber risk. The company's Aurora Platform, supported by the Concierge Security Team, operates as an extension of an organization's internal team, providing 24x7 monitoring, detection, and response. Arctic Wolf protects thousands of organizations worldwide from cyberattacks, helping mid-market and enterprise organizations detect and respond to threats across endpoints, networks, and cloud environments.
