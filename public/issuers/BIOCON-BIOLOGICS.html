Overview

Biocon Biologics is the world's leading fully integrated biosimilars company, serving over 6.3 million patients across 120+ countries by enabling affordable access to high-quality biologic medicines. Founded by Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw as part of Biocon Limited, the company specializes in diabetes, oncology, and immunology biosimilars. In December 2025, Biocon announced a $5.5 billion deal to fully integrate Biocon Biologics as a wholly owned subsidiary, including acquiring the remaining stakes from Serum Institute Life Sciences, Tata Capital, and Viatris. The biosimilars segment contributes 62% of revenue with 25% year-over-year growth. Biocon Biologics was honored with the Golden Peacock Award for Excellence in Corporate Governance 2025.
