Overview

Bloomreach is the leading AI-powered commerce experience platform serving over 1,400 global brands including Williams-Sonoma, Bosch, Puma, and Marks & Spencer. The company offers a suite of products including Discovery (AI-powered search and merchandising), Engagement (marketing automation), Content (headless CMS), and Clarity (conversational shopping). Having raised $422 million in total funding including a $175 million round led by Goldman Sachs, Bloomreach is positioned as a Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader for Commerce Search. In 2024, the company launched Loomi AI with seven premium features for personalization automation, partnered with Google Cloud and NVIDIA to enhance AI search capabilities, and announced its 2025 focus on making AI-powered personalization a revenue-driving reality for e-commerce.
