Overview

Celestial AI is a photonic semiconductor company revolutionizing AI infrastructure with its Photonic Fabric platform that enables unprecedented memory bandwidth by separating compute and memory using optical interconnects. Founded by David Lazovsky and headquartered in Santa Clara, California, the company's technology delivers faster AI processing with significantly better energy efficiency than traditional copper-based solutions. Celestial AI raised $520 million including a $255 million Series C1 at a $2.5 billion valuation in 2025. The Global Semiconductor Alliance named Celestial AI its 2024 'Start-Up to Watch.' In December 2025, Marvell announced its acquisition of Celestial AI for $3.25 billion to $5.5 billion, depending on revenue milestones.
