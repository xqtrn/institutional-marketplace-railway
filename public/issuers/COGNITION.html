Overview

Cognition is an applied AI company founded in 2023 by Scott Wu, Steven Hao, and Walden Yan, building the world's first fully autonomous AI software engineer. Their flagship product, Devin, can handle complete software engineering tasks from understanding requirements through implementation and deployment. Devin can write code, debug issues, deploy applications, and collaborate with human engineers on complex projects. In 2025, Cognition acquired Windsurf (formerly Codeium) to expand its AI coding product suite. The company serves category-defining enterprise customers including Goldman Sachs, Citi, Dell, Cisco, Palantir, Ramp, Nubank, and Mercado Libre, achieving explosive growth from $1M ARR in September 2024 to $73M by June 2025.
