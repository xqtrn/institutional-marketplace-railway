Overview

Degreed is an upskilling platform that connects learning to career opportunities, helping companies retain and reskill employees while supporting their career development. Founded in 2012 by David Blake with the mission of 'jailbreaking the degree,' the platform is now used by one-third of Fortune 500 companies. Degreed achieved unicorn status in April 2021 with a $153 million Series D led by Sapphire Ventures and Riverwood Capital at a $1.4 billion valuation. The company leverages AI technologies to bridge development gaps in team skills, focusing on targeted improvements and measurable outcomes.
