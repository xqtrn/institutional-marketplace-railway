Overview

Mercury is a business banking platform for startups, founded in 2017 by Immad Akhund, Jason Zhang, and Max Tagher. The company provides checking, savings, treasury, and venture debt through partner banks. Mercury reached $650M ARR in November 2025 with 10 consecutive quarters of GAAP profitability. The company serves 200,000+ businesses with transaction volume of $156B annually. In March 2025, Mercury raised $300M Series C at $3.5B valuation led by Sequoia. CEO Immad Akhund also runs a $26M angel fund.
