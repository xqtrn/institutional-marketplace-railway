Overview

Droom is India's first and largest technology-driven online automobile marketplace, enabling consumers and dealers to buy and sell new and used cars, motorcycles, scooters, and commercial vehicles. Founded in 2014 by Sandeep Aggarwal (who also founded ShopClues), Droom became a unicorn in 2021 at a $1.2B valuation. The platform hosts 1.1 million automobiles worth $15.7 billion in inventory from over 20,600 dealers across 1,105 cities in India. With 14 million app users, Droom has pioneered services like Orange Book Value (fair market pricing), History (vehicle inspection reports), and ECO (ecosystem for automotive services). Aggarwal is the only Indian entrepreneur to have founded two unicorns back-to-back.
