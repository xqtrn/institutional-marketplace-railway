Overview

Flexport is a digital freight forwarding and supply chain platform founded in 2013 by Ryan Petersen. The company provides technology-enabled logistics services including ocean, air, and truck freight. Flexport's valuation fell to $3.8B in 2024 (down from $8B peak in 2022) after freight rates normalized post-COVID. Revenue rebounded 30% to $2.1B in 2024 from a $1.6B low in 2023 (down from $5B peak in 2022). In 2023, Flexport acquired Shopify's logistics operations, making Shopify a 17% stakeholder. The company expects to be 'quite profitable' by end of 2025.
