Overview

Netlify is a web development platform founded in 2015 by Matt Biilmann who coined the term 'Jamstack'. The company raised $105M at $2B valuation in November 2021. Revenue reached $46.3M in 2024, up from $33M in 2023. Netlify celebrated 10 years since launch in February 2025. The platform competes with Vercel, Cloudflare Pages, and AWS Amplify. Investors include a16z, Kleiner Perkins, Menlo Ventures, and founders from GitHub, Heroku, Slack, and Figma.
