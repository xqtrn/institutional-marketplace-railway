Overview

Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited is India's largest mobile network operator and the third-largest globally with over 506 million subscribers. A subsidiary of Reliance Industries, Jio revolutionized India's digital landscape by launching with free 4G services in 2016, sparking a data consumption revolution. Today, Jio operates the world's largest 5G network outside China with 170 million 5G subscribers, and offers integrated digital services spanning connectivity, entertainment, payments, and enterprise solutions across 190+ countries.
