Overview

Dexterity is a robotics unicorn delivering full-stack Physical AI solutions for logistics, warehousing, and supply chain operations. The company's proprietary AI gives robots human-like dexterity to perform complex manipulation tasks including picking, packing, palletizing, and truck trailer loading. Dexterity serves global logistics leaders including FedEx, UPS, and GXO with robots that free workers from repetitive and strenuous tasks. The company has partnered with Sumitomo Corporation to deploy 1,500 warehouse robots across Japan by 2026, demonstrating the scalability of its Physical AI platform.
