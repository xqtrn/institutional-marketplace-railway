Overview

Linear is a modern issue-tracking and project management tool built for software development teams, founded in 2019 by former Airbnb, Coinbase, and Uber engineers. The company focuses on speed, simplicity, and the execution part of development. In June 2025, Linear raised $82M Series C led by Accel at $1.25B valuation after achieving 280% revenue growth. The platform has won marquee AI clients with its lean, user-centered product. Previous investors include Sequoia, founders of Slack, Vercel, and Supercell.
