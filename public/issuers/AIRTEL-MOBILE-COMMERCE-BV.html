Overview

Airtel Mobile Commerce BV (AMC BV) is the holding company for Airtel Africa's mobile money operations across 14 Sub-Saharan African countries. As Africa's fastest-growing mobile money platform, Airtel Money serves 49.8 million customers with digital wallets, microloans, savings, insurance, and international money transfers. The company processed $193 billion in annualized total payment value (TPV) in 2025, with fintech revenue growing 30.2% year-over-year. Valued at $2.65 billion after investments from The Rise Fund ($200M), Mastercard ($100M), and Qatar Holding, AMC BV is targeting a $4B+ valuation IPO in early 2026. The platform is a critical financial inclusion tool, with 44.6 million wallets connecting underbanked populations to essential financial services. Minority shareholders include The Rise Fund (8.05%), Qatar Holding (8.05%), Mastercard (4.02%), and Chimetech (1.96%), with Bharti Airtel International holding 77.89%.
