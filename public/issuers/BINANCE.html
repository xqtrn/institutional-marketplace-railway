Overview

Binance is the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume, serving over 275 million users globally. Founded in 2017 by Changpeng Zhao (CZ), Binance has grown into a comprehensive blockchain ecosystem offering spot and derivatives trading, staking, lending, NFTs, and more. Following a $4.3 billion settlement with U.S. regulators in 2023, Richard Teng took over as CEO, bringing his regulatory background from the Monetary Authority of Singapore and Abu Dhabi Global Market. Under his leadership, Binance has prioritized compliance, becoming the most licensed crypto exchange globally with 20+ regulatory approvals. In December 2025, Binance received full ADGM authorization in Abu Dhabi for exchange, clearing, and brokerage operations, establishing its global headquarters there. The company generated $16.8 billion in revenue in 2024, up 40% from the prior year.
