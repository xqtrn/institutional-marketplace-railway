Overview

AppDirect is the world's leading B2B subscription commerce platform, providing end-to-end solutions for technology providers and channel partners. Founded in 2009 in San Francisco, AppDirect offers marketplace development, billing, subscription management, and identity solutions. Named a leader in The Forrester Wave: Marketplace Development Platforms Q4 2024. In December 2024, AppDirect announced acquisition of Tackle.io to integrate hyperscaler marketplace listings with AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Also acquired Builtfirst in May 2024. Secured $100M from CDPQ in January 2024 for Capital Invest program. Total program funding reached $180M for financing technology advisors.
