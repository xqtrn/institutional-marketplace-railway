Overview

ecoSPIRITS is a Singapore-based circular economy technology company that has developed an innovative closed-loop distribution system for premium spirits and wine. Founded in 2018, the company's reusable packaging system decreases emissions by 60-90% while reducing costs for suppliers. ecoSPIRITS has secured global partnerships with industry giants Diageo and Pernod Ricard, with bars, hotels, and restaurants receiving spirits in 4.5L ecoTOTE containers designed to be used up to 150 times before being collected, cleaned, and refilled.
