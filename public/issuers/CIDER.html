Overview

Cider is a globally-minded, social-first fashion brand targeting Gen Z consumers, often described as a potential successor to SHEIN. Founded in 2020 by Michael Wang, Yu Oppel, and Fenco Lin, the company achieved unicorn status in just over a year with a $1 billion valuation backed by Andreessen Horowitz. Cider combines direct-to-consumer branding with SHEIN-style operations, using innovative community-building and real-time retail inventory management. Named one of Fast Company's 2024 Brands That Matter, Cider has offices in Guangzhou, Beijing, Los Angeles, New York, London, Seoul, and Brisbane.
