Overview

CloudWalk is a global payment network built on modern technology and proprietary blockchain, focused on revolutionizing the payment ecosystem for small and medium-sized enterprises. Founded in 2013, CloudWalk provides digital banking, loans, cashback cards, online sales, digital wallets, and payment terminals to over 1 million customers across 5,500+ Brazilian cities. The company achieved profitability in 2023 with $320.5 million in revenue and $22.3 million in profits. In 2024, CloudWalk scaled to $562 million in annualized revenue with nearly $1 million in revenue per employee, making it one of the world's most efficient fintechs. The company expanded to the U.S. in January 2024 with its Jim.com app.
