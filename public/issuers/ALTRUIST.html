Overview

Altruist is a modern custodian and brokerage platform for independent registered investment advisors (RIAs), founded in 2018 by financial industry veteran Jason Wenk. The Los Angeles-based company provides a vertically integrated platform combining custody, portfolio management, trading, and back-office capabilities. Altruist has grown rapidly, with AUM tripling for two consecutive years and triple-digit growth in revenue, brokerage accounts, and advisor adoption in 2024. The platform serves over 4,700 advisors and achieved 6.25% market share (doubled in 2025), positioning as a disruptive fourth player behind Schwab, Fidelity, and Pershing. The company raised $602M across 7 funding rounds and reached a $1.9B valuation after its April 2025 Series F.
