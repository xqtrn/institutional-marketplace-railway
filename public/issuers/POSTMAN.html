Overview

Postman is an API development platform founded in 2014 by Abhinav Asthana, Ankit Sobti, and Abhijit Kane. The company provides tools for API design, testing, documentation, and collaboration used by over 30 million developers. Postman raised $225M at $5.6B valuation in August 2021. Revenue grew to $313M in 2024 (up from $171M in 2023). The platform is used by 500,000+ companies including Microsoft, Stripe, and Salesforce. In January 2025, Postman launched AI Agent Builder for creating AI agents.
