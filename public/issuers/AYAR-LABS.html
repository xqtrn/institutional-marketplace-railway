Overview

Ayar Labs is pioneering optical interconnect solutions that use light instead of electricity to transmit data between chips, solving the critical bottleneck in AI data center infrastructure. The company's TeraPHY optical I/O chiplets and SuperNova light sources enable 8 Tbps bandwidth and 256 data channels, far exceeding traditional copper interconnects. In December 2024, Ayar achieved unicorn status with a $155M Series D backed by AMD, Intel, and NVIDIA. In 2025, the company unveiled the industry's first UCIe optical chiplet, enabling multi-vendor interoperability for AI scale-up architectures. Ayar partners with Corning, Ericsson, and Alphawave Semi on next-generation optical solutions.
