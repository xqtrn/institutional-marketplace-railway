Overview

Ada is a $1.2B AI customer service unicorn founded in 2016 by Mike Murchison and David Hariri in Toronto, named after computing pioneer Ada Lovelace. The platform has powered over 5.5 billion automated customer interactions for global enterprises including Meta, Square, YETI, Verizon, Canva, and Monday.com. Ada's AI-first approach enables companies to deliver extraordinary customer service at scale. The company reached $70.6M ARR in 2024 (up 22% YoY) with 6.3x generative AI adoption growth and 10x conversation volume surge. In January 2025, Ada appointed Sal Uslugil as CRO to lead the next phase of enterprise expansion.
