Overview

E-Life Financial Services (Yisheng Jinfu / ÊòìÁîüÈáëÊúç) is a travel-focused fintech platform under HNA Group, one of China's largest conglomerates and parent of Hainan Airlines. Founded in 2016, E-Life provides comprehensive financial services for travelers including foreign exchange, traveler's cheques, tax refund services, and consumer loans. The company operates as part of HNA Capital, leveraging the group's extensive travel and aviation network to offer integrated financial solutions. E-Life serves millions of Chinese outbound travelers with a suite of services designed to streamline cross-border financial needs.
