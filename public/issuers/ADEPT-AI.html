Overview

Adept AI is an AI startup building agents to automate software tasks, founded by former Google and OpenAI researchers. In June 2024, Amazon licensed Adept's technology and hired co-founders David Luan, Augustus Odena, and others for its AGI team. Zach Brock became CEO as Adept refocuses on agentic AI solutions with remaining team. Total raised: $414M at $1B valuation. Investors roughly recouped their investment in the Amazon deal.
