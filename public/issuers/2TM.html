Overview

2TM Group is the parent company of Mercado Bitcoin, Brazil's first and largest cryptocurrency exchange. Founded by brothers Gustavo and Mauricio Chamati in 2013, the company achieved unicorn status with $200M SoftBank investment in 2021 at $2.1B valuation. With 3.2M+ customers (equivalent to 80% of Brazil's stock exchange accounts) and $7B+ trading volume. Expanded into Europe with Mercado Bitcoin Portugal acquisition in 2022.
