Overview

Chrono24 is the world's leading online marketplace for luxury watches, reshaping the global watch market since 2003. The platform connects over 3,000 professional dealers and 30,000 private sellers listing more than 500,000 watches, attracting 20 million watch enthusiasts monthly from over 120 countries. The company became the first billion-dollar technology disruptor in the watch industry in 2021, backed by LVMH CEO Bernard Arnault's AglaÃ© Ventures and General Atlantic. In 2024, Formula 1 champion Charles Leclerc joined as an investor, underscoring the platform's appeal to high-net-worth watch collectors and industry insiders.
