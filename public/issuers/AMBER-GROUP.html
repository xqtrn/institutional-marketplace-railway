Overview

Amber Group is a global digital asset leader founded in 2017 by a group of Morgan Stanley quantitative traders including Michael Wu, Tony He, Thomas Zhu, and Wayne Huo. Headquartered in Singapore, the company provides integrated crypto financial services including trading, lending, and wealth management. Amber achieved $3B valuation in 2022 with a $200M round led by Temasek, Singapore's sovereign wealth fund. The company has processed over $1 trillion in cumulative crypto transactions and manages over $5 billion in digital assets. Amber operates globally with regulatory approvals in Australia, Switzerland, and the UK, serving both institutional and retail clients.
