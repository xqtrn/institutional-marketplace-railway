Overview

Arcadia is the leading clean energy technology company that makes renewable energy accessible to everyone. The company connects consumers and businesses to clean energy through its innovative software platform, offering community solar subscriptions, utility data access, and energy management tools. Arcadia's technology enables households to participate in community solar projects regardless of their home's suitability for rooftop panels, democratizing access to renewable energy across the United States.
