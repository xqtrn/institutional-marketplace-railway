Overview

Hippocratic AI is the global leader in safety-focused generative AI healthcare agents, founded in 2023 by CEO Munjal Shah alongside physicians, hospital administrators, healthcare professionals, and AI researchers from leading institutions including El Camino Health, Johns Hopkins, Washington University in St. Louis, Stanford, Google, and NVIDIA. The company's mission is to develop the safest Large Language Model for healthcare, bringing clinical abundance to address the growing shortage of 15 million healthcare workers worldwide. Named after the Hippocratic Oath, the company prioritizes "Do No Harm" above all else, focusing exclusively on non-diagnostic, patient-facing clinical tasks. As of November 2025, Hippocratic AI has achieved a $3.5 billion valuation following its Series C round, establishing partnerships with over 50 large health systems, payors, and pharma clients across 6 countries, completing over 115 million patient interactions with zero safety issues.
