Overview

CoinTracker is the leading cryptocurrency tax software and portfolio tracking platform, founded in 2017 by Jon Lerner and Chandan Lodha. The platform integrates with over 500 exchanges and wallets, automatically calculating capital gains, losses, and tax obligations for cryptocurrency traders. CoinTracker serves both retail investors and institutional clients, providing comprehensive reporting for IRS tax compliance including the new 1099-DA requirements starting in 2026. The company raised $100M at a $1.3B valuation in 2022 led by Accel, with participation from Coinbase Ventures, Intuit Ventures, and Y Combinator, positioning it as the go-to solution for crypto tax compliance.
