Overview

Augury is a leader in Industrial AI, providing machine health and predictive maintenance solutions that help manufacturers prevent downtime and optimize production. Founded in 2011, the company's AI-powered platform analyzes machine data to predict failures before they occur, improving reliability and reducing maintenance costs. Augury serves Fortune 500 manufacturers across food & beverage, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and other sectors, having analyzed over 500 million hours of machine data and generated over $1 billion in customer value.
