Overview

EGYM is a Munich-based fitness technology company that provides connected fitness solutions for gym operators, corporate wellness programs, and healthcare providers. Founded by Philipp Roesch-Schlanderer after experiencing frustration with ineffective gym equipment, EGYM has grown to serve over 18,000 fitness and health centers globally with 6 million monthly active users. The company's platform combines smart gym equipment with personalized training software and the EGYM Wellpass corporate benefit program connecting 17,000 sports partners with 14,000 corporate customers representing over 3 million eligible employees.
