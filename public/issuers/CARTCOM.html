Overview

Cart.com is a leading unified commerce and logistics platform that provides brands with the software, infrastructure, and expertise of the world's largest online retailers. Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Houston, Texas, the company has grown rapidly through nine strategic acquisitions including OceanX (Guthy-Renker's fulfillment arm) and Amify. Cart.com operates 18 omnichannel fulfillment centers totaling over 10 million square feet, with more than half being temperature-controlled. Serving 6,000+ customers and processing 75 million orders annually, Cart.com reached unicorn status with a $1.6 billion valuation in 2025.
