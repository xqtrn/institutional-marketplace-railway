Overview

Deliverect is a Belgian FoodTech unicorn providing a SaaS platform that integrates online orders from food delivery channels into restaurant POS systems. Founded in 2018 by Zhong Xu, Jan Hollez, Jelte Vrijhoef, and JÃ©rÃ´me Laredo, the platform connects restaurants with Uber Eats, DoorDash, Grubhub, Deliveroo, and more, automating order flow directly to the kitchen. Deliverect now powers over 55,000 restaurants across 52 countries, processing 450 million orders annually and targeting 800 million in 2025. The company achieved unicorn status in 2022 with $150 million Series D funding at $1.4 billion valuation, and revenue reached $44.7 million in 2024.
