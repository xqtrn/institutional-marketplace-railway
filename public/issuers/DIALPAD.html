Overview

Dialpad is an AI communications unicorn providing a unified platform for business phone systems, video meetings, messaging, and AI-powered contact centers. Founded by Craig Walker (creator of Google Voice), the company pioneered real-time transcription, automated note-taking, live sentiment analysis, and voice analytics. Dialpad's AI-native architecture turns every conversation into actionable insights, helping over 70,000 businesses make smarter decisions. The platform serves enterprises including Netflix, Uber, and WeWork with cloud-native communications that work from anywhere.
