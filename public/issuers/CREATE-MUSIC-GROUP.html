Overview

Create Music Group is an independent American music distribution, publishing, and data analytics company founded in 2015. The company operates Label Engine, a distribution platform serving over 75,000 artists and 5,000 labels worldwide. Create also owns Flighthouse, a Gen Z-focused digital entertainment brand with over 300 million followers across social media platforms. The company generates over 25 billion music streams each month on digital streaming platforms and has executed an aggressive acquisition strategy including Monstercat, Ostereo, and Enhanced Music. Create's platform provides artists with comprehensive analytics, monetization tools, and global distribution capabilities.
