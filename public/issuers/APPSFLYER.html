Overview

AppsFlyer is the global leader in mobile marketing analytics and attribution. Founded in 2011 in Israel by Oren Kaniel and Reshef Mann, AppsFlyer provides measurement, analytics, and fraud prevention for mobile marketers worldwide. The company reached $508M revenue in 2024, up from $400M in 2023, and serves 80,000+ customers including major brands. Valued at $2 billion in 2020, AppsFlyer is profitable and has not raised capital in 6 years. In late 2024, the company entered advanced acquisition talks with PE firms including Apollo Global and Fortissimo, with valuations estimated at $3.5-4.5 billion. CEO Oren Kaniel was exploring both strategic partnerships and a potential $300M IPO. AppsFlyer employs 1,600+ people globally.
