Overview

ByteDance Ltd. is a Chinese multinational technology company founded in 2012 by Zhang Yiming. The company operates a range of content platforms including TikTok (known as Douyin in China), Toutiao (news aggregator), Lark (enterprise software), and gaming divisions. ByteDance became the world's #1 social media company by revenue in Q1 2025, surpassing Meta with over $43 billion in quarterly revenue. The company's 2024 annual revenue reached $155 billion, with 75% coming from China. TikTok has 170 million US users and generates approximately $27 billion in international revenue. Despite regulatory challenges, ByteDance maintains a $330 billion valuation and continues aggressive expansion into AI, gaming, and enterprise software.
