Overview

Claroty is the industry leader in cyber-physical systems (CPS) protection, securing critical infrastructure across industrial, healthcare, and enterprise environments. The company's unified platform provides comprehensive visibility, risk and vulnerability management, threat detection, and secure remote access for operational technology (OT) and industrial control systems. Claroty works with 20% of the Fortune 100 and has achieved 300% growth in customers since 2020. The company surpassed $100 million in annual recurring revenue in 2023 and is preparing for a potential IPO at a $3.5 billion valuation.
