Overview

C2FO operates the world's largest on-demand working capital platform, connecting businesses to early payment opportunities and helping them unlock cash trapped in accounts receivable. Founded in 2008 by Sandy Kemper, former CEO of UMB Financial Corporation, the company has facilitated over $400 billion in lifetime funding to businesses worldwide. In 2024, C2FO achieved a record-breaking milestone by reaching $1 billion in daily funding for the first time and processing over 42 million invoices paid an average of 32 days early. The platform serves over 200 global enterprise clients, including six Fortune 10 companies, and has expanded significantly into emerging markets with an IFC partnership and RBI approval for India's TReDS platform.
