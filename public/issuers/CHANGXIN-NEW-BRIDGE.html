Overview

Changxin Xinqiao Storage Technology (Changxin New Bridge) is a DRAM memory chip manufacturing company within the ChangXin Memory Technologies (CXMT) family. Founded in 2021, the company operates as part of China's strategic initiative to build domestic semiconductor manufacturing capabilities. In June 2024, CXMT's controlling firm announced a $2.4 billion investment in an advanced packaging plant in Shanghai through related entities, which could be used to produce AI memory chips. The company contributes to CXMT's growing ~5% global DRAM market share and supports China's goal of semiconductor self-sufficiency amid ongoing technology restrictions.
