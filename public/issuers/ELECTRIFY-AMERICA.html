Overview

Electrify America is the largest open DC fast-charging network in the United States, established in late 2016 as part of Volkswagen's $2 billion, 10-year investment commitment following the diesel emissions settlement. The network now operates nearly 5,000 public chargers across almost 1,000 stations in North America, delivering over 600 GWh of energy in 2024 through 16 million charging sessionsâ€”a 50%+ increase from 2023.
