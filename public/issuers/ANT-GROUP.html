Overview

Ant Group is the world's largest fintech company and parent of Alipay, China's dominant mobile payments platform with over 1 billion users. Founded in 2004 as Alipay under Alibaba, the company was spun off as Ant Financial in 2014 and rebranded to Ant Group in 2020. Following regulatory restructuring, Ant Group's valuation recovered to $102.7 billion in 2025, up from $78.5 billion in 2023. The company achieved 61% profit growth in 2024 to $5.3 billion, driven by record R&D investment of $3.26 billion in AI. Ant International, the global unit generating $3 billion in revenue, is preparing for a potential Hong Kong IPO with valuations between $8-24 billion. In 2024, Jack Ma's control was formally diluted with 10 management staff receiving voting rights.
