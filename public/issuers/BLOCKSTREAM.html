Overview

Blockstream is a leading Bitcoin infrastructure company building the foundation for financial freedom. Founded in 2014 by Dr. Adam Back (inventor of Hashcash) and other prominent Bitcoin developers, Blockstream has developed key Bitcoin technologies including the Liquid Network sidechain and Core Lightning implementation. In October 2024, the company raised $210 million led by Fulgur Ventures to accelerate Layer 2 adoption and expand its Bitcoin treasury. With over $1.8 billion in assets issued on Liquid including stablecoins and tokenized securities, and 3,844 BTC locked onchain, Blockstream is central to Bitcoin's institutional adoption. The company expanded globally in 2025 with a research center in Lugano and new Tokyo office, launched Blockstream Asset Management for institutional investors, and spun out its mining and ASIC divisions as independent companies with over $350 million in combined funding.
