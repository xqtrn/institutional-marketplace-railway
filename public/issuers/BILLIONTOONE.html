Overview

BillionToOne is a leading molecular diagnostics company that has pioneered single-molecule counting technology for prenatal and oncology testing. Founded by Stanford PhD Oguzhan Atay, the company developed UNITY, the first prenatal test to include single-gene and carrier screening at no additional cost, capturing approximately 15% of the U.S. prenatal testing market. Their Northstar oncology platform provides liquid biopsy tests for guiding therapy in advanced cancer patients. BillionToOne went public in November 2025 with a $273M IPO that doubled in its debut, reaching a market cap exceeding $5 billion. The company has processed over 1 million patient tests and achieved $209M in trailing twelve months revenue with 84% year-over-year growth and emerging profitability.
