Overview

Turo is a peer-to-peer car sharing marketplace founded in 2009 by Shelby Clark. The platform connects car owners with people who want to rent vehicles, operating in 16,000+ cities with 350,000 active vehicles. Turo filed for IPO in January 2022 and updated filings through November 2024 but withdrew plans in February 2025. Revenue grew 9% to $958M in 2024 (down from 59% growth in 2022). The company achieved profitability in 2022 ($154.7M net income) but margins have compressed. In 2025, Turo partnered with Uber to list vehicles on Uber Rent.
