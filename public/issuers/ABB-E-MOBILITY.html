Overview

ABB E-mobility is the global leader in electric vehicle charging infrastructure, providing comprehensive charging solutions for homes, businesses, and public spaces. The company spun off from ABB in 2022 and has rapidly expanded its product portfolio to include the industry-leading Terra AC wallboxes, Terra DC fast chargers, and the revolutionary MCS1200 Megawatt Charging System for heavy-duty vehicles. In 2024-2025, ABB E-mobility launched the A400 charger which won the iF Gold Award 2025 for design excellence. The company serves major automakers, fleet operators, and charging network providers across Europe, North America, and Asia, with manufacturing facilities strategically located to serve global demand for clean transportation infrastructure.
