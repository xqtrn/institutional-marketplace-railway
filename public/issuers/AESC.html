Overview

Envision AESC is a global leader in electric vehicle battery technology and energy storage systems. Originally founded as a joint venture between Nissan and NEC in 2007, the company was acquired by Envision Group in 2019, with Nissan retaining a 25% stake. Today AESC powers over one million electric vehicles across 60+ countries for automakers including Nissan, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and Honda. The company has raised $2.4B from investors including GIC, Primavera Capital, and Hongshan Capital, targeting a $10B valuation. AESC operates gigafactories in Japan, UK, USA, China, and France, and ranks among the world's top 3 companies in energy storage cell shipments.
