Overview

Carta is the leading equity management platform helping private companies, venture capital firms, and their stakeholders manage cap tables, 409A valuations, fund administration, and liquidity. Founded in 2012 by Henry Ward as eShares, the company serves over 50,000 startups and 1.6 million security holders, managing more than $4 trillion in equity. Carta powers cap tables for 35% of all venture-backed startups and 84% of U.S. unicorns. After raising $1.16 billion including a $500 million Series G in 2021 at a $7.4 billion valuation, Carta has become the essential infrastructure for private market ownership.
