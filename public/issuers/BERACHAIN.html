Overview

Berachain is an EVM-identical Layer 1 blockchain built on Cosmos SDK, featuring a novel Proof of Liquidity (PoL) consensus mechanism that aligns network security with liquidity at the protocol level. The project launched its mainnet on February 6, 2025, after attracting $3.1 billion in liquidity on its pre-launch platform Boyco. Berachain uses a tri-token system: BERA for gas and transactions, BGT for governance, and HONEY as a stablecoin. Founded by pseudonymous team members known as Papa Bear, Smokey the Bera, and Dev Bear, the project evolved from the Bong Bears NFT community.
