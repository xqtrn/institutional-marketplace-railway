Overview

DevRev is an AI unicorn building an AI-native platform that unifies customer relationship management with product development. Founded by Dheeraj Pandey and Manoj Agarwal, former Nutanix executives, DevRev leverages large language models and autonomous AI agents to connect customer conversations directly to product engineering workflows. The AgentOS platform enables enterprises to deploy AI agents that handle customer support, automate ticketing, and provide developers with contextual customer insights. DevRev aims to replace legacy CRM systems like Zendesk and Salesforce with an AI-first approach to customer-centricity.
