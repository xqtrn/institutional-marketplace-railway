Overview

Awardco is the leading employee recognition and rewards platform that boosts employee productivity, reduces spend on disorganized rewards programs, and builds culture through value-driven recognition and rewards. As the first employee recognition solution to partner with Amazon Business, Awardco offers the largest reward network in the world with zero markups. The platform serves over 6 million users across 160+ countries and 3,000+ organizations including enterprise customers like AT&T, Adobe, Hertz, and Pacific Life. Founded in 2011 and headquartered in Lindon, Utah, Awardco quadrupled revenue since 2021 while maintaining cash-flow neutrality, achieving unicorn status in 2025.
