Overview

BitGo is the world's leading digital asset infrastructure provider, offering institutional-grade custody, wallet, and staking services. Founded in 2013 by Mike Belshe (co-inventor of the HTTP/2.0 protocol), BitGo pioneered multi-signature security for Bitcoin and has grown to custody over $100 billion in assets as of 2025. The company serves as custodian for USD1, the stablecoin from World Liberty Financial that was used in MGX's $2B Binance investment. BitGo filed confidentially for a US IPO in September 2025, seeking to become one of the first crypto-native companies to go public. With EU MiCA approval and operations in Korea (via Hana Financial) and Dubai, BitGo is the first independent custodian to surpass $100B AUC.
