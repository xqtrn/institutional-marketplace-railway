Overview

AutoRABIT is the leading provider of enterprise-grade DevSecOps solutions specifically designed for the Salesforce ecosystem. Founded in 2015, the company offers a comprehensive platform that enables organizations to automate CI/CD pipelines, secure code quality, manage data backups, and maintain regulatory compliance across their Salesforce environments. With solutions trusted by enterprises worldwide, AutoRABIT addresses the critical gap between productivity and security in low-code development. The company's acquisition of CodeScan in 2021 strengthened its security offerings, and recent FedRAMP milestones position AutoRABIT as a leader for government and highly-regulated enterprise customers.
