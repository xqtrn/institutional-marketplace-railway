Overview

Contentful is a headless content management system founded in 2013 by Sascha Konietzke and Paolo Negri in Berlin. The company pioneered the API-first CMS approach, enabling developers to manage and deliver content across any digital channel. Contentful raised $175M at $3B valuation in 2021 led by Tiger Global. Revenue reached $200M in 2024 with 4,000+ customers including 30% of Fortune 500 companies. In 2024, Contentful acquired Ninetailed for AI personalization capabilities.
