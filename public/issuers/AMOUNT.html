Overview

Amount is a unified banking technology platform spun out from online lender Avant in January 2020. Based in Chicago, the company provides digital lending, deposit account opening, fraud prevention, and risk management solutions for banks and credit unions. Amount achieved unicorn status in 2021 with its $99M Series D round. The platform serves financial institutions collectively managing over $3.1 trillion in assets and approximately 50 million U.S. consumers. In August 2024, Amount raised $30M to enhance AI/ML capabilities and accelerate credit union sector growth. In 2025, Amount was acquired by Fidelity National Information Services (FIS), a major payments processor.
