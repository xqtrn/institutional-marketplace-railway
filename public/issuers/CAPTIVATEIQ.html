Overview

CaptivateIQ is a San Francisco-based unicorn providing no-code sales commission management software. Founded in 2017 by Y Combinator alumni, the platform enables companies to design and automate customized commission plans without spreadsheets or legacy software. The company has transformed how enterprises manage incentive compensation, serving over 800 customers including more than 25% of the Forbes Cloud 100. Major clients include Affirm, Amplitude, Gong, Reddit, Udemy, and ClassPass. CaptivateIQ was named a Leader in the Forrester Wave for Sales Performance Management and a Gartner Peer Insights Customers' Choice vendor.
