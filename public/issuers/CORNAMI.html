Overview

Cornami (derived from 'tsunami of cores') is pioneering a breakthrough computing architecture that enables real-time Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE), allowing computation on encrypted data without decryption. The company's FracTLcore Computing Fabric scales from thousands to millions of cores, achieving 1,000,000x acceleration over conventional processors from NVIDIA and Intel. Backed by SoftBank Vision Fund 2 with $210M in funding, Cornami's technology addresses the trillion-dollar opportunity in data privacy, enabling secure AI, blockchain, and privacy-preserving machine learning workloads. The platform represents a fundamental breakthrough for quantum-resistant security.
