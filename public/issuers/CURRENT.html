Overview

Current is a consumer fintech company providing mobile banking services to underserved Americans through its partner banks, Choice Financial Group and Cross River Bank. Founded in 2015 by Stuart Sopp and Trevor Marshall, the company offers fee-free spending accounts, early paycheck access, overdraft protection, the Build Card for credit building, savings pods with up to 4% interest, and fee-free ATM access. With over 4 million accounts and no physical branches, Current delivers banking entirely through its mobile app, targeting Americans struggling with access to affordable liquidity and credit.
