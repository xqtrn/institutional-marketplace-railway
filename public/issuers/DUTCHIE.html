Overview

Dutchie is the leading cannabis technology platform in North America, powering over 6,500 dispensaries and processing more than $22 billion in annual cannabis sales. The company provides an all-in-one solution including point of sale systems, e-commerce, payments processing, and loyalty marketing tools for cannabis retailers. Founded in 2017, Dutchie reached a peak valuation of $3.8 billion in 2021 backed by prominent investors including Tiger Global, Thrive Capital, and celebrity backers Snoop Dogg (Casa Verde Capital), Kevin Durant, and former Starbucks CEO Howard Schultz. The company has consolidated the cannabis tech market through strategic acquisitions including Greenbits and Leaf Logix.
