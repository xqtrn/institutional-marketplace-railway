Overview

CoinDCX is India's largest cryptocurrency exchange, founded in 2018 by IIT-Bombay graduates Sumit Gupta and Neeraj Khandelwal. The platform enables users to trade over 500 crypto assets with deep liquidity and advanced trading features. CoinDCX has grown to serve more than 20.4 million users with over $165B in annualized transaction volume and $1.2B in assets under custody. The company expanded into the Middle East through its acquisition of BitOasis, and received strategic investment from Coinbase in October 2025, validating its position as the leading crypto platform for emerging markets. Despite India's challenging 30% crypto tax regime, CoinDCX continues to dominate the market.
