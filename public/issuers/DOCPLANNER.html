Overview

DocPlanner is Europe's largest healthcare booking marketplace, connecting patients with healthcare professionals across 13 countries in Europe and Latin America. Founded in Warsaw, Poland by Mariusz Gralewski, Lucjan Samulowski, and Luca Puccioni, the company has built a network of nearly 2 million healthcare professionals serving over 260,000 customers globally. DocPlanner processes more than 22 million patient appointments monthly through its marketplace brands including ZnanyLekarz (Poland), Doctoralia (Spain, Brazil, Mexico), and Jameda (Germany). The platform also provides practice management software for doctors and clinics.
