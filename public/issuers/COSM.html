Overview

Cosm is revolutionizing sports and entertainment viewing through immersive 360-degree LED dome venues that transport fans to the action. Each $80-90M venue features 87-foot domes with 8K LED screens, seating up to 2,000 guests. Formed through the merger of LiveLikeVR and C360, and acquisitions of Evans & Sutherland and Spitz Inc., Cosm has secured broadcast rights with the NBA, NFL, UFC, ESPN, NBC Sports, TNT, Fox Sports, and Amazon Prime Video. With venues open in Los Angeles and Dallas, and Atlanta announced, Cosm aims to open 50 locations globally by 2030. Backed by $250M from sports industry titans Marc Lasry, Dan Gilbert, and David Blitzer at a $1B+ valuation.
