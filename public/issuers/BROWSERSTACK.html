Overview

BrowserStack is the world's leading software testing platform, powered by AI to help developers and QA teams deliver quality software at speed. Founded in 2011 by Ritesh Arora and Nakul Aggarwal from IIT Bombay, BrowserStack achieved $4B valuation in 2021 and grew to $381M revenue in 2024. The platform runs 3 million tests daily for 7 million developers across 50,000 teams including Amazon, Microsoft, and NVIDIA. Named to Forbes Cloud 100 for five consecutive years, BrowserStack has expanded through acquisitions including Bird Eats Bug ($20M, 2024), Nightwatch.js (2021), and Requestly (2025). The company launched AI-based testing tools in 2025 to transform software development.
