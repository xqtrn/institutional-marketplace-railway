Overview

DANA (Dompet Anak Negeri) is Indonesia's leading digital wallet platform, serving over 200 million users nationwide. Founded in 2017 and launched in 2018 by former Alipay Indonesia executive Vincent Henry Iswaratioso, DANA enables QRIS payments, peer-to-peer transfers, and online/offline purchases. Backed by Ant Group (Alibaba), Sinar Mas Group, and Lazada Group, the company achieved unicorn status with a valuation of $1.2 billion. DANA processes an average of 10 million daily transactions and has become a pillar of Indonesia's digital economy, ranking as the country's second most valuable fintech startup.
