Overview

Chipper Cash is Africa's leading cross-border mobile payment platform, enabling free instant P2P money transfers across seven African countries: Ghana, Uganda, Nigeria, Tanzania, Rwanda, South Africa, and Kenya. Founded in 2018 by Ham Serunjogi and Maijid Moujaled, the company has grown to over 7 million users processing approximately 100,000 transactions daily. Chipper has expanded beyond payments to offer Chipper Cards (virtual and physical debit), Chipper Checkout (business payment gateway), and Chipper Stocks (fractional share trading). The company achieved unicorn status in 2021 at a $2B valuation, now valued at $1.25B making it Africa's fifth most valuable fintech.
