Overview

Akulaku is a leading digital banking and fintech platform in Southeast Asia, founded in 2016 by William Li. The company provides digital banking, BNPL, digital financing, and investment services to underserved customers in Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam, and Malaysia. Akulaku operates the Asetku wealth management platform and Bank Neo Commerce neobank. With a $2B valuation, it is Indonesia's most valuable fintech. Named to CNBC's World's Top Fintech Companies List in 2024 for innovation and impact in financial inclusion.
