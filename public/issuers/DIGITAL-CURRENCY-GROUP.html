Overview

Digital Currency Group (DCG) is a global enterprise that builds, buys, and invests in blockchain and digital currency companies. Founded by Barry Silbert in 2015, DCG has become one of the most influential investment firms in the cryptocurrency industry. The company owns major subsidiaries including Grayscale Investments (the world's largest digital currency asset manager), Genesis (crypto lending and trading), Foundry (Bitcoin mining and staking), and Luno (emerging markets crypto exchange). DCG has invested in over 200 blockchain-related companies including Coinbase, Ripple, Circle, Chainalysis, and many others. The firm reached a $10 billion valuation in 2021 before facing challenges from the crypto winter and Genesis bankruptcy in 2023. Founder Barry Silbert owns nearly 40% of the company.
