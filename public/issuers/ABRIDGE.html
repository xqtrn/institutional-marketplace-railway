Overview

Abridge is a $5.3B AI medical documentation company. Founded in 2018 by cardiologist Dr. Shiv Rao. Uses generative AI to transcribe and summarize clinical conversations. Deployed at Kaiser Permanente (24,600 physicians), Mayo Clinic, Johns Hopkins, Duke, and 150+ health systems. Named TIME100 Most Influential in AI 2024.
