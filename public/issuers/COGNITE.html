Overview

Cognite is a global industrial software company headquartered in Oslo, Norway, founded in 2016 by John Markus Lervik and Geir Engdahl. The company's flagship product, Cognite Data Fusion (CDF), is an industrial DataOps platform that contextualizes and connects operational data from various sources, making it accessible to humans and AI systems alike. Cognite serves major industrial companies including Shell, Equinor, Aker BP, and Saudi Aramco, helping them unlock value from their operational technology data. Listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange, Cognite was recognized as a Leader in Industrial Data Management by Verdantix in 2025, with SaaS ARR approaching $100M.
