Overview

Branch is the leading workforce payments platform enabling employers to offer fast, flexible payment options to both W-2 employees and 1099 contractors. Founded by Atif Siddiqi, Branch provides Earned Wage Access (EWA), paycards, digital banking, cashless tips, mileage reimbursement, and instant contractor payouts. In September 2025, Branch launched Branch Embedded, a solution allowing vertical SaaS platforms and gig marketplaces to integrate payments directly into their applications in weeks instead of quarters. Branch's suite includes the Branch App and Card digital banking account, White Label products, and Branch Direct. The company powers workforce payments for the nation's leading employers, enabling workers to access wages before payday and businesses to reduce turnover while unlocking new revenue streams through embedded finance.
