Overview

Decart is an Israeli AI company building real-time generative AI world simulators that can create playable interactive environments without traditional game engines. The company emerged from stealth in October 2024 with Oasis, the first playable real-time AI open-world model that replicates Minecraft gameplay through next-frame prediction, trained on millions of hours of footage. Unlike OpenAI's Sora which takes 10-20 seconds per second of video, Decart generates 20 frames per second with zero latency. Founded by Robert Wachen (Harvard) and Dean Leitersdorf (Technion), the company reached 1 million users in just 3 days and has raised $53 million from Sequoia Capital and Benchmark.
