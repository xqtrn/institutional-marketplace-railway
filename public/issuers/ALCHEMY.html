Overview

Alchemy is the leading Web3 developer platform, often called the 'AWS of blockchain.' Founded in 2017 by Stanford engineers Nikil Viswanathan and Joseph Lau. Powers over 70% of decentralized applications worldwide including NBA Top Shot, OpenSea, and Axie Infinity. Works with Coinbase, Stripe, JPMorgan, and Anchorage. In 2025, launched Cortex Engine reducing latency by 66%, acquired Bware Labs expanding Solana capabilities, and launched $5M Everyone Onchain Fund. Raised $564M total with $10.2B valuation.
