Overview

Chapter is a Medicare advisory platform that empowers seniors to maximize their Medicare benefits through greater choice and clarity while minimizing cost and complexity. The company provides personalized guidance to help Americans navigate the complex Medicare enrollment process and optimize their healthcare coverage. Co-founded by Vivek Ramaswamy, Chapter has grown 4x in enrollment over the past year and became a unicorn in April 2025. The platform combines AI-powered recommendations with human Medicare advisors to help beneficiaries understand their options across Original Medicare, Medicare Advantage, and supplemental plans.
