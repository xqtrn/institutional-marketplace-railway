Overview

Canva is an online graphic design platform founded in 2013 by Melanie Perkins, Cliff Obrecht, and Cameron Adams in Sydney, Australia. The platform democratizes design with drag-and-drop tools for creating presentations, social media graphics, documents, and videos. As of August 2025, Canva is valued at $65B with $3.3B ARR and 240M monthly active users. The company has been profitable for 8 consecutive years with 50%+ annual growth. AI tools are used 800M times per month (up 700% YoY). Canva acquired Affinity in 2024 for ~$380M. IPO expected in 2025-2026 with new CFO Kelly Steckelberg (ex-Zoom).
