Overview

Avant is a leading online lending platform that uses big data and machine learning algorithms to provide personal loans and credit cards to middle-income consumers who may be underserved by traditional banks. Founded in 2012 by Al Goldstein, John Sun, and Paul Zhang, the Chicago-based company has originated over $7 billion in loans and reached unicorn status with a $1 billion valuation. Avant pioneered the use of AI in credit decisioning, enabling faster approvals and more accurate risk assessment. In 2020, Avant spun off its fintech SaaS business unit as Amount, which was subsequently acquired by FIS, demonstrating the company's technology's value to the broader financial services industry.
