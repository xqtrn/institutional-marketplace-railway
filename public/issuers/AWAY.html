Overview

Away is the original travel lifestyle brand that launched in 2015 and achieved unicorn status in 2019. Known for its signature hardshell suitcases, Away has expanded into a full range of travel essentials including the revolutionary Softside collection launched in 2024. The company has evolved from direct-to-consumer roots to a multi-channel strategy, partnering with Amazon and Nordstrom to reach broader audiences. With its bags category growing 16% year-over-year in 2024, Away continues to innovate in the luggage space while building brand partnerships with organizations like the New York Liberty WNBA team. The company is headquartered in New York City.
