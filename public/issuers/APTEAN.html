Overview

Aptean is a leading provider of industry-specific enterprise software solutions serving manufacturing, distribution, and food and beverage industries. Formed in 2012 through the merger of CDC and Consona by Vista Equity Partners, Aptean has grown through aggressive M&A strategy, completing 54 acquisitions totaling approximately $1 billion in enterprise value. Under CEO TVN Reddy, the company generates over $650 million in annual revenue with approximately 4,000 employees. In July 2024, Vista Equity Partners fully exited its stake, with TA Associates, Insight Partners, Charlesbank Capital Partners, and Clearlake Capital Group continuing as investors. In 2025 alone, Aptean acquired Germanedge, Logility, and VlexPlus to expand its ERP and supply chain software capabilities across Europe.
