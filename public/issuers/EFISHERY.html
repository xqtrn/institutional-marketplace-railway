Overview

eFishery was an Indonesian aquaculture technology company founded in 2013 that became the world's first unicorn in the aquaculture industry. In December 2024, an internal investigation revealed massive financial fraud: the company had allegedly inflated revenue from $157M to $752M in the first nine months of 2024. Co-founders Gibran Huzaifah and Chrisna Aditya were suspended and later arrested. FTI Consulting now manages the company and has concluded eFishery is not commercially viable, recommending wind-down. Investors including SoftBank, Temasek, and Goldman Sachs face losses exceeding $300M.
