Overview

Ascend Elements is a leading provider of sustainable, closed-loop battery materials solutions, from EV battery recycling to commercial-scale production of lithium-ion battery precursor and cathode active materials. The company operates North America's largest electric vehicle battery recycling facility in Georgia and is building the Apex 1 facility in Kentucky, which will be North America's first sustainable cathode precursor manufacturing facility. Fast Company ranked Ascend Elements #1 in Automotive on the World's Most Innovative Companies list, and TIME named it one of America's Top Green Tech Companies.
