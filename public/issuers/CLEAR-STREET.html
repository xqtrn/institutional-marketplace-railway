Overview

Clear Street is a technology-driven prime brokerage modernizing capital markets infrastructure to improve market access for all participants. Founded in 2018, the company has built a cloud-native platform that provides clearing, custody, execution, and financing services to institutional investors and broker-dealers. Clear Street services over 700 institutional clients with approximately $16 billion in customer balances. Named to CNBC's World's Top FinTech Companies of 2024, Clear Street continues to expand globally, including joining the London Metal Exchange in 2024 as part of its European expansion.
