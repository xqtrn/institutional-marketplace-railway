Overview

Cyberhaven is a data security company that created Data Detection and Response (DDR), an innovative platform that protects enterprise intellectual property using pioneering data lineage technology. Unlike traditional DLP tools, Cyberhaven traces data from its origin through every movement, transformation, and fragmentation, providing context to accurately classify and protect sensitive data like source code, product designs, and customer records. The platform uses Linea AI with Large Lineage Models (LLiM) for autonomous risk detection, reducing false positives by 95% and Mean Time to Respond by 80%. The company serves enterprises globally from San Jose, California.
