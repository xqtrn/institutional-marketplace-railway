Overview

Clearco is a revenue-based financing platform that provides non-dilutive capital to ecommerce and SaaS businesses without requiring equity, personal guarantees, or blanket liens. Founded in 2015 by Michele Romanow and Andrew D'Souza, Clearco has deployed over $8 billion to more than 8,000 businesses globally. The company's funding model allows founders to receive capital from $10K to $20M to spend on growth initiatives like advertising, with repayment as a percentage of future revenue. After a significant recapitalization in 2023, Clearco continues to innovate with products like Receipt Funding for ecommerce businesses.
