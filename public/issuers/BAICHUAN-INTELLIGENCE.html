Overview

Baichuan Intelligence is one of China's leading AI startups, known as one of the four 'AI Tigers.' Founded in April 2023 by Wang Xiaochuan, the former CEO of Sogou (China's second-largest search engine), Baichuan has rapidly become the second-most valuable AI startup in China. The company develops large language models including Baichuan-7B, Baichuan-13B, and Baichuan 4, which ranks highest on SuperCLUE for Chinese language capabilities, outperforming GPT-4 Turbo and Claude 3 Opus in Chinese benchmarks. Baichuan is pioneering medical AI with Baichuan-M2Plus, called the 'Doctor's version of ChatGPT.' The core team includes AI experts from Google, Tencent, Baidu, Huawei, Microsoft, and ByteDance.
