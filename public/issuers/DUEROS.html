Overview

DuerOS is Baidu's conversational AI platform and the technology behind Baidu Smart Living Group (SLG), China's second-largest smart speaker ecosystem. Operating as a near-independent subsidiary valued at $2.9 billion after Series A funding in 2020, DuerOS powers over 400 million smart devices including the popular Xiaodu smart speaker line. The platform features full-duplex conversation mode, 200+ skill domains, and partnerships with major automotive brands including BMW, Daimler, Ford, Hyundai, and Kia. DuerOS competes directly with Alibaba's Tmall Genie and Xiaomi's XiaoAI, serving as China's answer to Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant.
