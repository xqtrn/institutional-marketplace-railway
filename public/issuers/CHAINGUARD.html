Overview

Chainguard is the safe source for open-source software, helping organizations build, deploy, and run applications with minimized risk from vulnerabilities and supply chain attacks. The company provides hardened base container images, language libraries, and virtual machine formats that are verified for security and updated continuously. Chainguard's approach dramatically reduces Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures (CVEs) in container images, helping enterprises meet compliance requirements while accelerating development velocity. Founded by former Google engineers who created key open-source security projects like Sigstore, Chainguard serves over 150 major customers including ANZ Bank, Canva, GitLab, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, Snap Inc., and Wiz.
