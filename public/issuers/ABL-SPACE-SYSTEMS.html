Overview

ABL Space Systems, rebranded to Long Wall in February 2025, pivoted from commercial launch to missile defense systems. Founded by SpaceX veterans Harry O'Hanley and Dan Piemont at MIT. After RS1 rocket setbacks in 2023-2024, the company now focuses on missile defense launch vehicles and hypersonic flight test vehicles for the Pentagon.
