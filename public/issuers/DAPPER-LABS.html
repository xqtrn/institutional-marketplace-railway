Overview

Dapper Labs is a Canadian blockchain company that created CryptoKitties, the first viral NFT project, and NBA Top Shot, one of the most successful mainstream blockchain applications. Founded in 2018 by Roham Gharegozlou, the company developed the Flow blockchain specifically designed for consumer applications and digital collectibles. NBA Top Shot, developed with the NBA, has generated $1.2 billion in total sales with 1.6 million users. Notable investors include Michael Jordan, Kevin Durant, a16z, Coatue, and Google Ventures. The company has partnered with major entertainment brands including the NFL, UFC, and Disney Pinnacle.
