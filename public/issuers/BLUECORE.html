Overview

Bluecore is a leading retail marketing technology company that unifies identity, behavior, and product signals to deliver personalized experiences across email, SMS, site, and paid media. Founded in 2013, the company reached unicorn status in August 2021 with a $125 million Series E at a $1 billion valuation, bringing total funding to $225 million. Bluecore's AI platform goes beyond product recommendations to serve as a stylist, trendspotter, review summarizer, and affinity predictor, increasing purchase likelihood by 2x. In November 2024, Bluecore acquired Alby for Gen AI Shopping Assistant technology. The company's Customer Growth Benchmarks Report found retailers with highest identification rates see 53% higher repeat purchase rates. Major partnerships include Fender and DXL, with the Shopping Agent Platform enabling brands to deploy conversational AI with custom voice and brand values.
