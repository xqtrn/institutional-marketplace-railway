Overview

Apollo.io is the leading go-to-market platform for revenue teams, combining sales intelligence and execution workflows with a B2B buyer database of over 270 million verified contacts. Founded in 2015 by Y Combinator alum Tim Zheng, Apollo grew 900% since 2021 and reached $134M ARR in 2024, up 40% from $96M in 2023. The company became a unicorn in 2023 with a $1.6 billion valuation. Apollo serves over 3 million users across 500,000 companies including Qualtrics, Customer.io, and Census, with 40,000+ paying customers. Named to Forbes Best Startup Employers 2024 and ranked #125 on Deloitte Technology Fast 500. The fully distributed team of 500+ spans 30 countries.
