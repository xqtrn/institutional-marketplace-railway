Overview

Earnix is a leading AI-powered pricing and decisioning platform for insurance and banking organizations, founded in 2001 by Sammy Krikler and Yoni Cheifetz in Israel. The cloud-based platform combines artificial intelligence with advanced analytics to enable real-time modeling, simulations, and personalized price and product offerings. Earnix achieved unicorn status in 2021 after raising $75 million at a $1 billion valuation. The company serves major insurance carriers and retail banks globally, helping them optimize pricing strategies while maintaining regulatory compliance. In 2025, Earnix acquired Zelros to expand its AI capabilities in the insurance space.
