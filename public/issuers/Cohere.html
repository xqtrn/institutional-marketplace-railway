Overview

Cohere is a Canadian artificial intelligence company founded in 2019 by former Google Brain researchers, including Aidan Gomez, co-author of the transformative "Attention Is All You Need" paper. The company specializes in developing enterprise-focused large language models and AI platforms that prioritize data security and privacy. Unlike consumer-facing competitors, Cohere focuses exclusively on B2B solutions, offering cloud-agnostic, customizable AI that can be deployed on-premises or in private clouds. As of September 2025, Cohere has achieved a $7 billion valuation and serves hundreds of enterprise clients including Oracle, RBC, McKinsey, and government agencies worldwide.
