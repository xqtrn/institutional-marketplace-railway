Overview

Avathon, formerly SparkCognition, is a leader in Industrial AI that helps the world's largest companies maximize uptime of capital-intensive assets, enable efficient manufacturing ramp-up, and ensure worker safety. Founded in 2013 by Amir Husain and rebranded in October 2024, the company has built a pioneering AI platform serving oil and gas supermajors, aerospace OEMs, global retailers, CPG manufacturers, and renewable energy operators. Backed by Temasek Holdings, Verizon Ventures, and Boeing, with strategic partnerships with NVIDIA and Qualcomm, Avathon relocated its headquarters from Austin to Silicon Valley to accelerate its platform expansion and is tripling its India workforce to 400 people over two years.
