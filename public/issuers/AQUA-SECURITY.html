Overview

Aqua Security is the largest pure-play cloud native security company, providing end-to-end security for cloud native applications. Founded in 2015 in Israel by Dror Davidoff and Amir Jerbi, Aqua secures containerized, serverless, and cloud VM workloads. In January 2024, Aqua raised $60M in funding extension at a valuation above $1 billion, bringing total funding to $325M. The company serves 500+ enterprise customers including 40% of Fortune 100 and 6 of the top 10 North American banks. Revenue grew to $89.9M in 2024, up from $56.3M in 2023. Aqua is the only company providing full lifecycle security from code to cloud.
