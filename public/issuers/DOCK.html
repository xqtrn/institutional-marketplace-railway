Overview

Dock is a decentralized identity platform that enables organizations to issue, manage, and verify digital credentials using blockchain technology. Founded in 2017 by Nick Lambert and Elina Cadouri, Dock has built a comprehensive ecosystem for reusable digital identities that are portable, private, and verifiable. The platform launched its mainnet in September 2020 with a fixed supply of 1 billion DOCK tokens. In 2024, Dock merged networks with cheqd to accelerate decentralized identity adoption and partnered with Socure to expand its digital ID capabilities. The company's solutions help organizations reduce fraud, streamline verification processes, and give individuals control over their personal data.
