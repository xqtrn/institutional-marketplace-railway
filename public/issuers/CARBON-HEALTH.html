Overview

Carbon Health is a San Francisco-based healthcare unicorn combining urgent care, primary care, and virtual care through a tech-enabled platform. Founded in 2015 by Udemy co-founder Eren Bali and physicians Caesar Djavaherian and Tom Berry, the company operates a hybrid model with physical clinics and telehealth services. Carbon Health expanded rapidly during COVID-19, reaching a $3.3B peak valuation before restructuring as pandemic revenue declined. The company partners with CVS Health to pilot offerings in select stores and has served millions of patients across its clinic network.
