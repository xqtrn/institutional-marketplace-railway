Overview

CircleCI is the world's largest shared continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD) platform, processing over 1 million builds daily for software teams worldwide. Founded in 2011 by Paul Biggar and Allen Rohner, the company helps development teams ship code faster with intelligent automation, security scanning, and release orchestration. CircleCI achieved unicorn status in 2021 with a $1.7 billion valuation, backed by leading investors including Greenspring Associates, IVP, and Sapphire Ventures. The platform delivers proven 664% ROI for customers and acquired Vamp for release orchestration capabilities.
