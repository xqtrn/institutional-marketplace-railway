Overview

Alloy is an identity risk management platform that helps financial institutions streamline compliance and fraud prevention. Founded by Tommy Nicholas, Charles Hearn, and Laura Spiekerman, the company provides a centralized dashboard integrating multiple data sources for KYC and AML compliance, automated identity verification, and fraud detection. Alloy became a unicorn in 2021 after raising $100M Series C at $1.35B valuation, followed by a $52M extension in 2022 at $1.55B. The company's 2024 revenue reached $42.4M (up 72% from $24.7M in 2023) with over 200 enterprise customers including major banks and fintechs.
