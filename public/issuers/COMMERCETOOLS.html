Overview

commercetools is the pioneer of headless commerce, founded in Germany and now headquartered in Munich. The company provides an API-first, cloud-native commerce platform that enables enterprises to build flexible, composable digital commerce experiences. commercetools pioneered the MACH architecture (Microservices, API-first, Cloud-native, Headless) that has become the industry standard for modern commerce. The company serves major global brands and has been named a Leader in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for Digital Commerce five years in a row. With $175M in 2024 revenue and a $1.9B valuation, commercetools is on track for an IPO in 2025-2026. In February 2025, the company underwent layoffs as the e-commerce market normalized from pandemic-era growth rates.
