Overview

CAIS is America's leading fintech platform for alternative investments, providing financial advisors with streamlined access to hedge funds, private equity, private credit, real estate, structured notes, and digital assets. Founded in 2009 by Matt Brown, who spent 30 years at the intersection of wealth management and technology, CAIS became a unicorn in 2022 after raising $225 million led by Apollo and Motive Partners. The platform has facilitated over $21 billion in transaction volume and supports more than 32,000 advisors. In 2025, CAIS launched an AI-powered tool that transforms hours of fund research into minutes of actionable insights, further cementing its position as the first truly open marketplace for alternative investments.
