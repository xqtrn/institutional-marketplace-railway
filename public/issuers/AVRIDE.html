Overview

Avride is a leading developer of autonomous driving technologies headquartered in Austin, Texas. Originally spun off from Yandex's self-driving group, the company is now a subsidiary of Nebius Group. Avride develops and operates both autonomous vehicles (robotaxis) and sidewalk delivery robots, sharing technologies between both platforms for mutual advancementâ€”a unique approach in the industry. The company's robotaxi fleet uses Hyundai Ioniq 5 electric vehicles equipped with Avride's self-driving system, while its delivery robots fulfill orders for hundreds of restaurants through partnerships with Uber Eats. Avride maintains R&D centers in Tel Aviv, Belgrade, and Seoul, enabling global innovation in autonomous mobility.
