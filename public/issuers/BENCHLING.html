Overview

Benchling is the leading R&D cloud platform for the life sciences, enabling biotechnology companies to accelerate drug discovery and development. Founded in 2012 by MIT alumni Sajith Wickramasekara and Ashu Singhal, Benchling provides cloud-based bioinformatics tools for molecular biology research. The platform is used by over 1,300 biotech customers across biopharma, agriculture, industrial biotech, and consumer packaged goods, including over 200,000 scientists worldwide. Benchling reached $210M ARR in 2024 and was named to the Forbes Cloud 100 for the third consecutive year.
