Overview

Chint Anneng Digital Energy is China's largest rooftop distributed photovoltaic company, a subsidiary of Chint Electric focused on residential solar power for rural households. Founded in 2015, the company provides comprehensive solutions including survey, design, installation, and after-sales operation of rooftop PV systems. With over 800,000 cumulative users and a market share exceeding 30%, Chint Anneng has become the dominant player in China's household solar market.
