Overview

CARS24 is one of India's largest used car marketplaces and one of the four major organized players in India's used car segment. Founded in 2015 in Gurgaon by IIT Bombay alumni Vikram Chopra (CEO), Mehul Agrawal, Gajendra Jangid, and Ruchit Agarwal, the platform enables consumers to buy and sell pre-owned vehicles. CARS24 has raised over $1.3 billion from investors including SoftBank, Alpha Wave Global, and DST Global, achieving a $3.2B valuation. The company expanded internationally to UAE, Thailand, and Australia in 2021. Recent restructuring efforts focus on core business profitability.
