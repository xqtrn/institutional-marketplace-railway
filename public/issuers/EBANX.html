Overview

EBANX is a leading payment platform connecting global companies with consumers in emerging markets across Latin America, Africa, and Asia. Founded in 2012 in Curitiba, Brazil by JoÃ£o Del Valle, Alphonse Voigt, and co-founders, EBANX became Brazil's first southern-region tech unicorn in 2019 after investment from FTV Capital. The company serves over 1,600 global digital businesses including Microsoft, Sony, TikTok, Coinbase, Uber, AliExpress, and Ubisoft, processing payments across e-commerce, gaming, SaaS, streaming, and travel verticals. In 2021, Advent International made a $430 million investment in EBANX, representing their largest investment in a Latin American technology company.
