Overview

Entrata is a comprehensive property management software platform serving the multifamily housing industry. Founded in 2003 by Johnny Hanna in Lehi, Utah, the company provides an all-in-one platform for property accounting, marketing, leasing, facilities management, and resident services. Entrata has grown to serve over 20,000 customers and achieved $503M in revenue in 2024. The company received a $200M minority investment from Blackstone at a $4.3B valuation, with Silver Lake as a key investor through $707M in total funding.
