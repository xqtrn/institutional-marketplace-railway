Overview

1inch Network is a leading decentralized exchange (DEX) aggregator founded in 2019 at ETHGlobal hackathon by Sergej Kunz and Anton Bukov. The protocol finds optimal swap routes across 400+ liquidity sources to get users the best trading rates. 1inch raised $190M at a $2.25B valuation in its 2021 Series B led by Amber Group.
