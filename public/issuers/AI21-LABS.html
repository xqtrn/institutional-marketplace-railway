Overview

AI21 Labs is a Tel Aviv-based generative AI unicorn building foundation models and enterprise AI systems. Founded in 2017 by Stanford Professor Yoav Shoham, serial entrepreneur Ori Goshen, and Mobileye founder Amnon Shashua ($15.3B exit to Intel), the company has raised $636M from NVIDIA, Google, Intel Capital, and Walden Catalyst. AI21's flagship Jamba model family is built on a hybrid Mamba SSM-transformer architecture with 256K context windows, ideal for complex enterprise tasks like document analysis and research. In 2025, AI21 launched Maestro, an agentic orchestration platform that improves LLM accuracy by 50% while providing full observability and reliability guarantees. The company's models are available on AWS Bedrock, Azure, Google Cloud, and Amazon VPC, serving thousands of enterprises across industries.
