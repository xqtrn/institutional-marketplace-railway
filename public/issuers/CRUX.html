Overview

Crux Climate is a capital markets platform for the clean economy, founded in 2023 by Alfred Johnson (former Deputy Chief of Staff at US Treasury) and Allen Kramer. The platform facilitates the transfer and monetization of clean energy tax credits created by the Inflation Reduction Act, fundamentally transforming how decarbonization projects are financed in the United States. Crux estimates $30 billion of tax credit transfers occurred in 2024, with projections of $55-60 billion for 2025. The marketplace connects clean energy developers with corporate buyers seeking to purchase transferable tax credits, creating liquidity and price transparency in this emerging market.
