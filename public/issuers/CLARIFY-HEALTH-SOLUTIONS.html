Overview

Clarify Health is building healthcare's system of intelligence, generating over 300 million annual patient journeys and 18 billion AI-powered predictions that empower providers, health plans, and life sciences companies to make better care decisions. The company unites longitudinal data from government and commercial claims, electronic health records, and prescriptions to deliver actionable insights for value-based care. Clarify's platform enables healthcare organizations to understand patient journeys, optimize network design, and improve outcomes across the care continuum.
