Overview

Diamond Foundry is a clean tech unicorn producing lab-grown diamonds using zero-emission plasma reactors powered by renewable energy. The company grows single-crystal diamonds for jewelry and semiconductor applications, offering an ethical and sustainable alternative to mined diamonds. Diamond Foundry's technology creates gem-quality diamonds atom by atom in specialized reactors, with each diamond certified carbon neutral. Backed by Leonardo DiCaprio, Twitter founder Evan Williams, and iPod creator Tony Fadell, the company is expanding production in Spain with an $85M EU grant to reach 20 million carats annually.
