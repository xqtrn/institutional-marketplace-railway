Overview

Anduril Industries is a defense technology company founded in 2017 by Palmer Luckey (Oculus VR founder) and Brian Schimpf. The company brings Silicon Valley talent and capital to the defense sector, building autonomous systems, AI-powered software, and advanced weapons for the U.S. military and allies. Anduril's revenue hit $1 billion in 2024 (up 138% YoY) with new government contracts exceeding $1.5 billion. The company has won major programs including a $9 billion autonomous aircraft contract with General Atomics, a $642 million Navy contract, and took over Microsoft's $22 billion IVAS augmented reality headset project. Valued at $30.5 billion following a $2.5 billion Series G led by Founders Fund in June 2025, Anduril is one of the 13 most valuable private companies globally.
