Overview

CoinList is the leading platform for token sales and crypto community building, spun out of AngelList and Protocol Labs in 2017. The platform has facilitated compliant token launches for some of the most successful crypto projects including Filecoin, Solana, Flow, and Mina, enabling over $105M in token purchases in 2024 alone from investors across 170 countries. CoinList combines regulatory compliance expertise with deep crypto-native community reach, averaging over 10,000 participants per token sale. With 13 of 14 sales oversubscribed in 2024, including the PEAQ sale that generated 8.5x returns, CoinList remains the gold standard for projects seeking to launch tokens and build communities responsibly.
