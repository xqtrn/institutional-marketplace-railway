Overview

Contentsquare is the global leader in digital experience analytics, helping over 1,300 brands including Walmart, Samsung, and Microsoft understand how customers interact with their websites and apps. The AI-powered platform captures and analyzes user behavior to optimize digital experiences and increase conversion rates. Founded in 2012 in Paris by Jonathan Cherki, Contentsquare has raised $1.4B at a $5.6B valuation, making it one of France's most valuable tech unicorns. The company acquired Hotjar in 2021 and continues to expand through strategic M&A including Loris in 2025.
