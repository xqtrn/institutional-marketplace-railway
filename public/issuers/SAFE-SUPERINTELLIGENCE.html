Overview

Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI) is an AI research company co-founded by Ilya Sutskever, Daniel Gross, and Daniel Levy in June 2024 with a singular mission: building safe superintelligence. Sutskever, a legendary figure in AI who co-founded OpenAI and led the development of GPT models, became CEO in July 2025 after Gross departed for Meta. SSI reached a staggering $32 billion valuation in April 2025 after raising $2 billion led by Greenoaks Capitalâ€”a 6.4x valuation increase from its $5B September 2024 round. The company has raised $3 billion total from Alphabet, NVIDIA, Andreessen Horowitz, Sequoia, DST Global, and Lightspeed. Operating with only ~20 employees, SSI has no products, no revenue, and no commercial ambitionsâ€”it exists solely to solve the superintelligence alignment problem. In April 2025, Google Cloud announced a partnership to provide TPUs for SSI's research.
