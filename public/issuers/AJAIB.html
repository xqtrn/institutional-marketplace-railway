Overview

Ajaib is Indonesia's leading financial super app founded in 2018 by Anderson Sumarli, Yada Piyajomkwan, and Kevin Lee. The platform enables Indonesians to buy and sell stocks, ETFs, and mutual funds with an affordable and accessible interface. Ajaib achieved unicorn status in 2021 with a $153M Series B led by DST Global that valued the company at $1 billion, becoming Indonesia's first unicorn investment app. The company reached 3 million retail investors by mid-2023, up 50% from 2022. Ajaib is one of the largest brokerages by transaction volume in Indonesia and is backed by Y Combinator, DST Global, Ribbit Capital, and Horizons Ventures.
