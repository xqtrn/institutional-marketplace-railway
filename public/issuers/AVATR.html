Overview

Avatr Technology is a premium Chinese electric vehicle brand jointly developed by Changan Automobile, CATL, and Huawei. Originally founded in 2018 as Changan-Nio, the company rebranded as Avatr in 2021 after NIO's withdrawal, evolving into a luxury EV manufacturer combining Changan's manufacturing expertise, CATL's advanced battery technology, and Huawei's intelligent driving systems. Avatr vehicles feature the Huawei ADS 3.0 autonomous driving system with multiple LiDAR sensors, establishing the brand as a technology leader in China's competitive EV market. With $1.5 billion raised in Series C funding in December 2024, Avatr is preparing for a Hong Kong IPO targeting 2026.
