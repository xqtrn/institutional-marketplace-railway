Overview

BGL Group is one of the UK's largest digital distributors of insurance and financial services, best known as the owner and operator of Compare the Market, the country's leading price comparison website. Founded in 1992 by Douw Steyn as Budget Insurance Company, BGL has grown into a multi-billion pound enterprise serving millions of UK consumers. In 2022, BGL sold its insurance underwriting business to Markerstudy, refocusing on its digital comparison and distribution platforms. Under CEO Mark Bailie, who joined in September 2020, the company plans to triple its size by expanding into new financial services categories. BGL Group has raised $906 million in funding from CPP Investments and is valued at $3.2 billion.
