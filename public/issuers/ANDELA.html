Overview

Andela is a global tech talent marketplace connecting companies with vetted software engineers from 135+ countries. Founded in 2014, Andela achieved unicorn status with a $1.5B valuation after $200M Series E in 2021. About 60% of contractors are in Africa and Latin America. Clients include Mastercard, GitHub, Goldman Sachs, and ViacomCBS. In August 2024, former Uber exec Carrol Chang became CEO. Forrester study shows 97% ROI, 66% faster hiring, and 33% faster project delivery.
