Overview

Boxabl is a modular housing company revolutionizing construction with its foldable Casita homes that can be mass-produced on automotive-style assembly lines. The flagship 361-square-foot Casita starts at $60,000 and ships flat to unfold on-site in hours. The company announced a SPAC merger with FG Merger in 2025, valuing Boxabl at $3.5 billion, with expected Nasdaq listing under ticker BXBL. Despite over 170,000 customer reservation deposits representing $10+ billion in indicated demand, Boxabl has delivered approximately 285 Casitas while scaling its Las Vegas factory to 4,000 homes annual capacity. Key 2025 regulatory approvals include California full climate zone modular approval and Nevada residential code compliance. The company also announced the Baby Box RV unit starting at $19,999 and made a Bitcoin treasury purchase in May 2025.
