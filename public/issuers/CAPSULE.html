Overview

Capsule is a New York City-based digital pharmacy that achieved unicorn status in 2021 with a $1 billion valuation after raising $300 million in Series D funding led by Durable Capital Partners. Founded in 2015 by Eric Kinariwala, Capsule has reimagined the pharmacy experience with same-day prescription delivery, seamless refills, and integrated healthcare services. The company partners with doctors, hospitals, and insurance providers to streamline prescription management and improve medication adherence. Capsule's platform handles everything from coordinating with prescribers to managing insurance claims, delivering medications directly to patients' doors. Backed by major investors including Thrive Capital, TCV, T. Rowe Price, and Baillie Gifford, Capsule continues to expand its one-stop-shop digital health ecosystem.
