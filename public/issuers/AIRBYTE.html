Overview

Airbyte is a San Francisco-based open-source data integration platform and unicorn, building the leading ELT tool for moving data from APIs, databases, and files to data warehouses, lakes, and lakehouses. Founded in 2020 by Michel Tricot (ex-LiveRamp) and John Lafleur (serial entrepreneur) during Y Combinator W20, Airbyte has grown to 600+ connectors and achieved unicorn status with a $1.5B valuation in December 2021. The platform offers both self-hosted and cloud-hosted options with AI-powered connector creation that generates integrations from API documentation in seconds. Key 2024-2025 milestones include Airbyte 1.0 release, Apache Iceberg support, HIPAA/GDPR compliance features, and recognition in Fast Company's Most Innovative Companies 2025. Backed by Altimeter Capital, Coatue, Benchmark, and Accel, Airbyte powers data movement for AI and analytics workloads with enterprise-grade security, RBAC, and self-healing pipelines.
