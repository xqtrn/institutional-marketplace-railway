Overview

China UnionPay Merchant Services (UMS) is a subsidiary of China UnionPay, China's national card network, providing comprehensive merchant payment services nationwide. Incorporated in 2002 with headquarters in Shanghai, UMS enables merchants to accept UnionPay-labeled cards and provides a broad spectrum of financial services to cardholders and businesses. The company has grown into one of China's largest payment processors, serving millions of merchants across the country with POS terminals, mobile payments, and integrated financial solutions as part of the UnionPay ecosystem that rivals Visa and Mastercard globally.
