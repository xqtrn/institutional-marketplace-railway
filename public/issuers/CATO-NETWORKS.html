Overview

Cato Networks is the pioneer of SASE (Secure Access Service Edge), delivering a single cloud-native platform that converges SD-WAN, a global private backbone, and complete network security. Founded in 2015 by cybersecurity legends Shlomo Kramer (co-founder of Check Point and Imperva) and Gur Shatz, Cato enables enterprises to securely connect any user, site, cloud, or application anywhere in the world. With 46% ARR growth in 2024 and over 3,500 enterprise customers, Cato raised $359 million in Series G funding in June 2025 at a $4.8 billion valuation, bringing total funding to over $1 billion. The company is considered a leading IPO candidate in cybersecurity.
